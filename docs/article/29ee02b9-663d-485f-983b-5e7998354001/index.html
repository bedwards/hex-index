VIGIL Tour Dates!

By George Saunders · Story Club ·Oct 16, 2025 · 3 min read

Hi everyone,

I’m very happy and excited to be able to announce tour dates for Vigil, my new novel, which comes out on January 27, 2026.

The links below will take you to the host’s websites, for tickets. Please note that not all of the ticket-sales links may be live yet - the host’s website will let you know when tickets will become available. Also, we’ll be announcing the moderators for the events in the coming weeks.

I can’t wait to get out there and support this book, which is, I think, the craziest I’ve ever written, and meet some of you (many of you, I hope!) in-person. I’ll be doing signings at most events, and that’s always a nice chance to say hello.

Please feel free to pre-order Vigil here.
