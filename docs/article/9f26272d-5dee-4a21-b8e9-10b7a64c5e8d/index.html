  1. “Gatherings are the creation of a temporary alternative world.” Priya Parker and I had a nice chat about the creative power of Halloween, gathering, jokes, pranks, and other creative mischief. (Don’t miss my notes underneath the video — they’re more intelligent, probably, than anything I said.)

(I love to listen to Anne Enright read it in her Irish accent.)
