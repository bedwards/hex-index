“Publishing a book takes a village!” ~ Craig Jackson

Happy New Year–Week One, Indie Authors and Curious Creatives!

The new year is zooming right along. I haven’t even made my vision board yet! I’ve hit the ground running with working on getting the Bookworm Orchard bookstore ready to open in a month! I’ve always been up for a good challenge, and this is proving to be the best one in a while. We have already filled all the author book signing spots for our Grand Opening event on April 25th.

Several local authors will be joining us including: Angela Herrington, April Decton, Ava Cuvay, Bess Sturgis, Bob Freeman, Christine Lawrence, Connor Freeman, Elizabeth Woodrow, Erin Hayden, Jana Strickler, Jessica Miller, Laura Petrucce, Liz Flaherty, Lori Goss-Reaves, Mark Saluke, Mona Mehas, Pilar Del Rey, Rochelle Bradley, Shae Smith, Stephanie Renee, Terra Chism, and Valeria Horton.

We are excited to host the first of hopefully many book-signing events for indie authors and our book-loving community!

I’m also still working on the Stories From The Orchard anthology and Kickstarter campaign. The goal is to start the Kickstarter pre-launch on January 20th, so hopefully, I will have the link for you by the next update. The purpose of the anthology is to raise start-up funds for our Bookworm Orchard bookstore that will be located at Boston Hill Center, 428 S Washington St, Suite 101, Marion, Indiana.

We will have our first author group meeting at Bookworm Orchard: Saturday, January 24, 2026, 10 AM, to talk about consignments, events, and other book marketing opportunities. I hope you can join us!

Featured Author: Craig Jackson

Craig “C.S.” Jackson is an 11-year sober man on a mission to help the loved ones of addicts and alcoholics. He lives in New Hampshire, loves being involved with his two “screenagers,” playing “old man” basketball, and surfing (even in the winter in NH!). Now he adds writing to his favorite hobbies. Welcome to the writer world, Craig. It’s addictive in the best way!

Craig is in the process of launching his debut book Beyond the Lies. The ebook is available now and the paperback will be available January 20th. He already has the manuscript written for the second and third book in this series!
