  • Jonestown 17 min read

    Directly relevant to the nominated book 'Death in the Jungle: Murder, Betrayal, and the Lost Dream of Jonestown' by Candace Fleming. The Peoples Temple and the 1978 mass murder-suicide is a significant historical event that readers may know superficially but not in depth.

  • Robert Smalls 23 min read

    Subject of the nominated book 'Defiant: The Story of Robert Smalls' by Rob Edwards. Smalls was an enslaved African American who commandeered a Confederate ship during the Civil War and later became a U.S. Congressman - a remarkable historical figure whose story provides rich educational context.

The nominations have been tallied and your 2025 finalists for The Stackies are here. Thanks to everyone who nominated books, we had our largest turnout for nominations ever, and that is reflected in the wide ranging list of books below. The finalists are the five books that received the most votes in each category.

Voting will open up on January 6th for all paid subscribers to Unstacked and run through January 12th. The winners of this year’s Stackies will be announced on Friday, January 16th.
