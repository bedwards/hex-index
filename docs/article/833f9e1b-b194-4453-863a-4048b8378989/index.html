Author Feature: Chris McWhirt

Chris McWhirt is from Fort Wayne, Indiana. He is married with five grown children and two dogs: Buddy and Lucy. Chris has written three books along with short stories on Booksie and other websites. He is also a contributor to our Stories From The Orchard anthology!

His nonfiction book, Glory Daze, is about sandlot baseball. Last November Chris made his debut entry into the Sci-fi world with book one of his Adamic Chronicles trilogy: Intervention. He recently published book two in the trilogy: Deception.
