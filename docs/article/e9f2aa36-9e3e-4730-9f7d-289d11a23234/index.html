  • Rusticle

  • Compartment (ship)

    The text explains the 'tug of war' between the sinking bow and the buoyant stern, a dynamic directly caused by the design and failure of the ship's watertight bulkheads.

  • White Star Line

    The excerpt notes the ship was registered in Liverpool because its owners were the White Star Line, a specific rival to the Cunard Line that is central to the company's history but less known than the Titanic itself.
