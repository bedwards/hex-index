📝 Last Week in Longevity

  • BioAge announces the launch of their 2nd clinical trial --reversing immune system aging in COVID-19 patients. AI/ML drug discovery startup BioAge has officially begun its trial of BGE-175 for COVID-19 patients over the age of 60. BGE-175 is a small molecule antagonist of the prostaglandin D2 DP1 receptor, which regulates immune function. BGE-175 is a drug licensed from Shionogi, a Japanese pharma company.
