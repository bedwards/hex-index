Humility

By Bridger Tower

Humility is the antidote to pride. Humility is not feeling less than anyone else around you. Humility is recognizing and embracing your dependence upon the Savior, Jesus Christ.

One of my favorite quotes on humility is “Humility is not thinking less of yourself, it's thinking of yourself less.” – C.S. Lewis
