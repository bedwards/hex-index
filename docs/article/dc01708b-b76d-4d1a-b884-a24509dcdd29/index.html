  • Genetically modified mouse

    The article relies on the specific mechanism of inserting human ACE2 receptors into mice to model human infection, a technical concept central to the study's 100% mortality claim.

  • RaTG13

    While the speaker mentions a pangolin virus, the actual scientific context for SARS-related coronaviruses in China revolves around the RaTG13 strain and the debate over its pangolin origins versus bat origins.

  • Encephalitis

    The speaker highlights the unusual and fatal progression of the virus from respiratory symptoms to late-stage brain infection, making the medical definition of viral encephalitis essential context.
