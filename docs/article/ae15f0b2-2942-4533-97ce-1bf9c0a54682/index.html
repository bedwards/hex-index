🗳️ Lenny’s Polls: How do you feel about your job right now?

We’ve been thinking about how much collective wisdom there is in this community, and how much more we can learn from one another’s day-to-day experiences. So we’re going to experiment with capturing more of it and sharing what we learn with you.

This week, we’re launching Lenny’s Polls!

These are short polls that we’ll run every couple of weeks, to get a pulse on what’s happening in your work lives.

First up, we’re asking: How do you feel about your job right now?

The poll takes about a minute. Just 5 questions. Participation is 100% anonymous.

Take the poll here: https://web.polly.ai/bbqv1d.

This will only work if you participate, so please take the quick poll. And if you do, we’ll share the early results with you before we share them with the public. Thank you for being part of it!
