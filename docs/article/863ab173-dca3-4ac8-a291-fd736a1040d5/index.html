Merry Everything Indie Authors and Curious Creatives,

I hope you are enjoying the holidays! This Fresh Insights features indie author Chris Stolle and a Book Marketing Recap of 2025 Featured Authors answering one of the most popular questions, “What helps you sell the most books?”

Before we dive in, I want to give a quick update on the Stories From The Orchard anthology, Kickstarter campaign, and Bookworm Orchard bookstore.

I’m working on setting up the Kickstarter campaign now and plan to pre-launch the Stories From The Orchard anthology in January. The purpose of the anthology is to raise start-up funds for Bookworm Orchard which is an indie author bookstore that I, along with two other local authors, plan to open at Boston Hill Center in Marion, Indiana.

An author meeting will be held at Bookworm Orchard, 428 S Washington St, Suite 101, Marion, IN, on Saturday, January 24, 2026, 10 AM, to talk about consignment and event opportunities.

We are planning a soft opening February 13, 2026, 5-8 PM, during Boston Hill’s Galentine celebration.

Submissions to the Stories From The Orchard anthology is closed. Thanks to all the contributors who will receive a lifetime subscription to Indie Author Insider!
