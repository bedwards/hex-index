This month on Cosmic TV (07-25)

By James Maynard · The Cosmic Companion ·Jul 6, 2025 · 9 min read

July will see yet another outer planet changing signs, introducing us to the vibes of the “fire and air era” of the coming years, in which “spirit” (fire) and “mind” (air) are going to be the central evolutionary drivers. With Uranus’s entry into the air sign of Gemini on July 7, we will not only have all of the slow-moving planets in air or fire, but we’ve also got Saturn, Uranus, Neptune and Pluto all placed at the very first degrees of their respective zodiac signs, forming a triangle (blue lines below) pointing to the Aries Point, the very beginning of the zodiac – a very rare constellation indeed:

Uranus entering Gemini on July 7, 2025, forming a talent triangle (blue lines) with Pluto in Aquarius and the Saturn-Neptune conjunction on the Aries Point

The symbolic meaning of this pointer-shaped constellation involving so many ‘beginnings’ isn’t hard to interpret. It's like the cosmos saying, “hey, guys, this is not a new chapter. This is the very first page of a whole new book!”

Another thing that is really peculiar about this triangle formation (and which only becomes clear at a second glance) is that it affects the first degrees of all zodiac signs in some way or another. So if you have planetary placements anywhere at the beginning of any sign, the larger collective developments tend to play a major role in your personal life at the moment. Those with planets at the beginning of the fire and air signs will be especially tuned in to the current zeitgeist and will probably be more open to whatever the future holds in store for them and willing to change and experiment. Those with placements at the beginning of Aries, Cancer, Libra and Capricorn might feel more resistance as they are called to take a major step in a maturation process. Those with placements at the beginning of Gemini, Virgo, Sagittarius and Pisces could be in for a surprise or an unexpected turn of events, whereas those with placements at the beginning of Taurus, Leo, Scorpio or Aquarius are invited to a longer process of weeding out what’s outlived its purpose.
