I started my travels by following this list of beautiful stations on the Visit Stockholm website.

I had seen articles about the art on the Stockholm Metro before, and there were always about a dozen stations featured, so I figured that these were the only nice ones. But the reality is that 90% of stations on the metro system have some kind of sculptures, paintings, light installations, tile mosaics, or other kinds of artwork. I am not exactly sure what criteria was used to decide on this particular list of stations. Perhaps some of the artists were more famous than others, but I visited over two dozen metro stations while I was in Stockholm, and many of these were just as beautiful as the ones on the visit Stockholm website.
