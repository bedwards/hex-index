Antrhopic Reaction

  • CEO Dario Amodei

    • Issued a statement refusing to accede, stating the company cannot in good conscience remove safeguards for the two specified uses, which they view as unreliable or ethically risky with current technology.

    • He discussed the feud on CBS News.
