Happy Friday friends – a quick note that for today’s newsletter I’m mixing it up a bit…

Seeing as we’re deep into fall, today I’m sharing a compilation of all my best autumnal recipes + gardening tips from the past 3 years of this newsletter. Sometimes even I forget about the HEAPS of hyper-seasonal recipes + growing resources that I’ve shared here over the years, and TBH they are too dang good to overlook. So let’s have a quick refresh, shall we?

(Full disclosure that this weekend I’m on my first truly *solo trip* away from home since our son Sawyer was born over 4 years ago… what?! I’m back in my stunning old stomping grounds in Santa Barbara, CA, where Matthew and I lived for a very, very blissful season of life in our mid 20’s.)

I’ve gotta be honest that it’s been an unexpectedly FULL and sleep-deprived fall season back home in North Carolina, and this tired farmer needs a little reset that I’m hoping the California sunshine and Pacific air can provide. Thanks for hanging in there with me, I’m genuinely excited to be back in full force next week, and I’ll be sharing a BANGING butternut squash, black bean & beef chili recipe that I’ve finally perfected after lots of testing. Trust that it will be worth the wait.
