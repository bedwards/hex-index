The Final “Eno” Art House Day!

To help make screenings for smaller art houses more feasible, over the past year we’ve offered “Eno Art House Days” where dozens of cinemas in North America, Australia, the UK and elsewhere collectively show a new version of the film. The final installment of this program is on Wednesday, September 17. Art House Day is for paid public events only, and not applicable for film festivals, free events or institutional screenings. The list of cities and theaters will be announced soon. Want to get a screening in your city? Tell your local indie theater to go here for info.
