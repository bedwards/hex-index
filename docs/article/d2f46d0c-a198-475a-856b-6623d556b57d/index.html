Authoritative PLA texts published since 2022 emphasize enhancing the aggressiveness of the military strategic guidelines, noting that the PLA must take the initiative to seize strategic opportunities and create favorable external conditions. On 29 December, just as the summit between President’s Zelenskyy and Trump was finishing at Mar-a-lago, and hundreds of journalists were filing their initial analyses of the meeting, the Peoples Liberation Army Eastern Theatre Command announced that it had commenced exercise "Justice Mission 2025" in the Taiwan Strait.

Similar to other joint exercises conducted in 2025 and 2024, this was an activity to rehearse future military campaigns to “shield” Taiwan from outside military forces that might come to its aid. At the same time, the bottom of the shields in the PLA posters for the exercise are shaped like blades. This is intended by the Chinese to “cut off the delusional dream of Taiwan independence.” Thus, Justice Mission 2025 is intended to project the image of an advanced, capable PLA that can both attack Taiwan while fending off assistance from the military forces of America, Japan and elsewhere.

The exercise is rehearsing a range of military activities and features live fire drills.

The exercise is one of President Xi’s ways of demonstrating his displeasure at the 17 December 2025 announcement by the U.S. government of a massive weapons sale to Taiwan. With a value of US$11.1 billion, this is the largest single U.S. sale to Taiwan in history, and includes highly symbolic weapon systems such as the HIMARS long-range precision strike system.

Finally, we should not discount that Justice Mission 2025 is also designed to message China’s continuing displeasure with Japan. Two of the exercise zones for this PLA activity would (theoretically) permit operations against Japanese military targets on Yonaginajima to the east.
