FAQ

By Kahlil Greene

How do I subscribe?

To subscribe to this newsletter, go to historycanthide.substack.com/subscribe.

There are three subscription types:

  1. Monthly - $5 a month

  2. Annual - $50 a year

  3. True Investigator - You select the amount (>$150 a year)

The website default selects the annual plan, but if you want to contribute more, select True Investigator and input an amount in the submission box (see below).

I want to make a one-time donation. How do I do that?

To make a one-time donation, you can send me money through Venmo or CashApp.

To do that, you can find me on Venmo (TheGenZHistorian) or on CashApp ($kahlilgreene00).

How do I unsubscribe?

To unsubscribe, go to historycanthide.substack.com/subscribe and choose the unsubscribe feature.

I want to contact you for a business or editorial inquiry?

To contact me for a business matter or an interview, please fill in the form at kahlilgreene.com/contact-me
