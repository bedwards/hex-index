It's Dieter's Birthday, Watch "Rams" Free!

By Gary Hustwit · Gary Hustwit's Films ·May 19, 2025 · 3 min read

Tomorrow, May 20th, Dieter Rams turns 93 years old. And it’s become a tradition that every year on Dieter’s birthday we stream my documentary “Rams” free to viewers worldwide. You may have seen it already, but here’s a chance to revisit this film about one of the most influential designers on the planet, with an original soundtrack by Brian Eno. Spread the word to friends who might be interested! The film is free to stream today and tomorrow with subtitles in a dozen languages. Happy Birthday Dieter!
