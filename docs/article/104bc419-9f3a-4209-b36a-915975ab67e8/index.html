Northeast Autumn Harvest Guide

By Isabella

Root Vegetables

Earthy, sweet, and perfect for roasting or mashing:

  • Carrots

  • Beets

  • Turnips

  • Parsnips

  • Rutabagas

  • Radishes

Squashes & Gourds

Rich, creamy, and comforting—perfect for soups and grain bowls:

  • Butternut squash

  • Acorn squash

  • Spaghetti squash

  • Kabocha squash

  • Pumpkin

  • Delicata squash

Greens

Nutritious, hearty, and versatile:

  • Kale

  • Cabbage

  • Brussels sprouts

  • Broccoli

  • Cauliflower

  • Collard greens

Alliums

Aromatics that add depth and warmth to any dish:

  • Onions

  • Garlic

  • Shallots

  • Leeks

Other Seasonal Veggies

Delicate and unique flavors to round out your autumn meals:

  • Sweet potatoes

  • Celery root

  • Fennel

  • Spinach

  • Winter radicchio

Tips for Enjoying Your Autumn Harvest:

  • Roast root vegetables with olive oil, salt, and a sprinkle of cinnamon for cozy warmth.

  • Steam or sauté greens with garlic for a quick, nourishing side.

  • Make creamy soups with winter squash and fresh herbs for a seasonal centerpiece.

  • Visit your local farmers market or CSA to experience the freshest, most flavorful produce.
