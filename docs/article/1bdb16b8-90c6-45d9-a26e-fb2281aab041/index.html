two men ended up arguing and crownford drew his pistol and threatened to kill hoes who retaliated by flinging his axe hitting cranford in the head and killing him instantly hoes fled the following day newspapers ran the story some reported that hoes had also sexually assaulted cranford's wife and even tried to murder their baby the atlanta constitution informed its readers that when hose is cooled he will be either lynched and his body riddled with bullets or he will be burnt at the stake it continued that there have been whisperings of burning at the stake and of torturing the fellow and so capture one local said that if hoses on earth are never rest easy until he's caught and burned alive and that's the way all of us feel a few days later on the 23rd officers found an arrested hose
