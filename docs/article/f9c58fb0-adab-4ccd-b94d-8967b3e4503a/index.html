How to Simplify an 18-Field, 2-Column Form

By Anthony Tseng

A form with eighteen fields and two columns is a dreadful sight to see. It’ll send users running for the exit and abandoning your app. What looks overwhelming to them isn’t only the number of fields, but the multi-column layout.

With two columns, users have to scan vertically and horizontally to fill out the form. The fields are overpopulated on the left and right sides. It’s almost as if they have to fill out two forms in one. In their minds, they think this is going to be a lot of work, and they don’t have much time. As a result, they change their mind about completing the form, and you lose another customer.
