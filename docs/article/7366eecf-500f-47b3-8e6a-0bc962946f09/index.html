Hello, hello, my dear space lovers! 

Let me be completely honest: I don’t feel like writing this newsletter today.

I just wanted to share my feelings with you, take a moment to reflect on this moment in history, and keep moving forward. 

Take care, enjoy your day and talk to you soon 

Cheers from sunny Spain, 

Juan
