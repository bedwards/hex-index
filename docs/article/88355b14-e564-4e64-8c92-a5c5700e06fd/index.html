Ukraine Dispatch 3: An Interview with Nataliia

By Mick Ryan · Mick Ryan ·Oct 18, 2025 · 2 min read

Welcome to my Ukraine Dispatches, updates from my latest visit to Ukraine. This is my third dispatch. My first one, which covered Ukrainian military training as well as a certain incident with a snake, is available to read at this link. My second one is available here.

My visit to Ukraine is going well, and I have been able to meet with Ukrainian military officials as well as a range of other folks as part of my research into the war in Ukraine, and developments in modern war.

Today’s update is a short one. It has been a long day in southern Ukraine but I wanted to get this update out before I hit the sack.

On my second night in Kyiv, I was interviewed by Natallia Lutsenko. Nataliia has interviewed me a couple of times before. She is always a pleasure to speak with because her background research for interviews is superb and our discussions are long enough to tackle a range of topics.

In our latest conversation, for the Ukrainian War & Politics 24 channel, we discuss:

  • The Trump-Putin phone call.

  • The Trump-Zelenskyy meeting in Washington DC.

  • Support for Ukraine’s war effort.

  • Long range strikes.

  • The trajectory of the war.

Thanks for following my visit to Ukraine. In my next update, I will cover a visit to military units in southern Ukraine.
