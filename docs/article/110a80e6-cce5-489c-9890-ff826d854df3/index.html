I am pleased to confirm that We Have Never Been Woke is now officially published by Princeton University Press.

Now that the book’s out, I’m hitting the road to talk about it in the U.S. and abroad. Book tour information available here. If I’m not headed to your neck of the woods yet, reach out to my booking agent, Molly Grote (Molly_Grote@press.princeton.edu), and let’s change that! I want to talk to as many people as I can in as many places as I can to think and talk through these issues together.

If, by chance, your local book store does not have the hardcover in stock, you can also snag a copy via Bookshop.org, Thriftbooks, Barnes & Noble, Amazon and through most other major retailers. You can also pick up a copy directly from the publisher via the Princeton University Press website.1 Some used copies may also be available on AbeBooks.

For folks who prefer e-books, you can get a copy through Kobo, Google Play, Amazon, Barnes & Noble, and beyond.

If audiobooks are your jam, you can get that version, narrated by me, through Apple Books, Barnes & Noble, Audible, Google Play, Libro.fm and Kobo.

For those who want to read the book but are unwilling or unable to purchase a copy, information on how to get the book in your preferred format via local or college libraries is available here.

Ultimately, wherever you are, however you’d like, in whatever format you prefer, and through whatever means make sense — get your hands on a copy ASAP! I’m biased, of course, but I think it’s really a great book.
