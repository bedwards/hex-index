Explore these related deep dives:

  • Embodied cognition

    The article argues that China's path to AGI diverges from Silicon Valley's software-centric view by prioritizing physical-world interaction, making this technical distinction central to understanding the geopolitical divergence.

  • Swarm intelligence

    Explicitly cited in China's 15th Five-Year Plan as a key field for AGI development, this concept explains the 'bottom-up' collective approach the article contrasts with the 'top-down' Manhattan Project model favored in the US.

  • Recursive self-improvement

    The article hinges on the debate between this theoretical 'intelligence explosion' mechanism and China's more constrained, embodied alternative, defining the core strategic difference in how each nation envisions the path to superintelligence.
