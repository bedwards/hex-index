The answer, it's complicated. Just ask Nashville attorney William Growth, a lawyer, a man trained in the finer points of evidence. Growth left his Honda Civic at a boat ramp in 2023, tossed his wallet and jacket into a river like it was CSI midlife crisis, and vanished. Police launched a full-scale search, divers, dogs, helicopters, emergency responders.

But the thing is, Growth wasn't dead. He was hiding in a motel in Montana. 5 days later, he called the cops pretending to be his own killer. Voice analysis revealed the killer was Growth himself.

He later checked into a psychiatric facility, which may have been the first smart legal move that he had made in quite some time. So, here's the big question.
