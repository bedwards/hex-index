Hi there!

I want to try something different for The Palindrome.

Instead of running a typical “discount for a few days” sale, I’d like to turn the next month into the time where we take The Palindrome to the next level instead. Think of it as a crowdfunding campaign, right here on Substack.

What could you help build if your subscription is turned into more math & ML for everyone?

It’s no secret that I want to turn The Palindrome into a full educational platform, focused on the foundations of mathematics and machine learning. I’ve been

  • talking with a couple of educators about exclusive mini-courses and video courses for The Palindrome,

  • thinking a lot about how to bring a lot more visuals, more structure, and more depth to what we already do,

  • and even bring full-time creators to the team.

Here’s the plan. (And a 30% off forever discount.)

The 2026 Milestone & Rewards Plan

Over the next month, every new paid subscription will move us closer to concrete milestones.

Currently, we stand at 550 paid subscribers. If we reach these milestones by the end of 2025, I’ll reinvest directly into The Palindrome:

  • 600 paid subscribers → Exclusive mini-course (Q2 2026)

    A focused, paid-members-only mini-course on a core math/ML topic (think: a deep dive you can follow step-by-step, not just a single article). The topic of the first mini-course will be “Mathematics of Machine Learning”.

  • 700 paid subscribers → Another exclusive mini-course (Q4 2026)

    A second mini-course with a different expert/angle. The idea is to slowly build an extensive “course library” for members.

  • 800 paid subscribers → Dedicated Manim animator

    We’ll bring on a Manimator so more articles come with clean, high-quality animations that make the concepts instantly click.

  • 1,000 paid subscribers → Full-time writer

    This is the big one. With a full-time writer on the team, we can ship more deep dives, more courses, and more structured learning tracks throughout 2026.

Every one of these rewards benefits all paid members.

If you upgrade now, you’re not just supporting what exists today; you’re helping shape what we can build next year.
