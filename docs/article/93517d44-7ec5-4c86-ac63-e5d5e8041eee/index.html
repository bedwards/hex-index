Deep Dives

Explore these related deep dives:

  • History of geology

    Goudal explicitly grounds her work in the evolution of how natural philosophers from antiquity to the Renaissance hypothesized the Earth's formation.

  • Stratigraphy

    The artist mentions printing backdrops with different 'stratus' layers, directly referencing the geological method of analyzing rock layers to understand Earth's history.

  • Tidal bore

    Her series 'The Observers' required working with tides, making the specific mechanics of tidal movements and their constraints essential to understanding her process.
