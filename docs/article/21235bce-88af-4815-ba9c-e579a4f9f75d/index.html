The humanities face challenges on multiple fronts. Political pressures from both right and left have transformed education into a vocational training exercise, while ideological orthodoxy has replaced traditional aesthetic inquiry. This decline extends beyond academia, with literacy rates dropping and technological disruption threatening intellectual traditions.

First, let's give a fair assessment of why the humanities are in decline, especially in the universities in the US.
