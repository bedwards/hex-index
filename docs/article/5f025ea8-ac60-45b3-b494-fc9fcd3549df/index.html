Vote Now for The Best Lit Mags of 2025

By Benjamin Davis

Every year, our community votes for their favorite lit mags in the Best Lit Mag Awards. Guess what time it is?

Winning lit mags receive $300 each and get showered with love & attention across social media & Substack.
