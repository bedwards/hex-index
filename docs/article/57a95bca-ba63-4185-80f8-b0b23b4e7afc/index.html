  • Unincorporated association

    The DUNA is explicitly described as a digital optimization of this centuries-old common law entity, and understanding its traditional limitations regarding liability and contract enforcement reveals why a new statutory framework was necessary for blockchain networks.

  • Limited liability limited partnership

    Since Wyoming pioneered the LLC to unlock business innovation, examining this specific 1977 statute provides the historical blueprint for how the state is now attempting to replicate that success for decentralized organizations through the DUNA.

  • Legal person

    The article hinges on granting decentralized networks the ability to sue, be sued, and sign contracts, a concept that challenges the traditional requirement for a centralized human agent to hold legal rights and obligations.
