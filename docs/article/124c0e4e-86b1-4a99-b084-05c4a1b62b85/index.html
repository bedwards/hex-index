Deep Dives

Explore these related deep dives:

  • The Great Adventure of Horus, Prince of the Sun

    This film serves as the specific crucible where Miyazaki first developed the 'image board' technique, illustrating how the chaotic, collaborative struggle of this production shaped his later visual philosophy.

  • Yōichi Kotabe

    As a fellow artist on Horus whose cleaner style contrasted with Miyazaki's frantic sketches, Kotabe's work provides the necessary counterpoint to understand the unique, energetic function of Miyazaki's rougher concept art.

  • Yasuo Ōtsuka

    Otsuka's mentorship and his specific observations on Miyazaki's drawing speed reveal the generational transmission of animation techniques that allowed Miyazaki to evolve from a sketcher into a director.
