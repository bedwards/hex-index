Happy December! Happy holidays! Happy frantic wild chaotic stressful time of year!

This year my kids are 4 and 2, and it’s the first holiday season where they’re old enough to be properly giddy about Christmas trees and ornaments and making cookies and hot chocolate and stories of Santa’s reindeer… and it’s the actual BEST. I’m someone who usually gets very stressed out by the holidays (though I’m pretty sure that is all of us?), so it’s been a real breath of fresh air to re-experience the magic of this season through the eyes of my kids.

Out on the farm, there’s still tons to do as we end the year, but thankfully there’s no sense of intense urgency like there is during the peak growing season. We still have crops to clear out, weeds to pull, tarps to move, and a giant mess of a barn to clean out… but my team and I are moving more slowly and gently, sleeping in a little, and we know we will get it all done eventually.

I have a LOT of exciting updates and projects in the works for 2026, which I’ll be able to share more about in the coming weeks, so stay tuned. :)
