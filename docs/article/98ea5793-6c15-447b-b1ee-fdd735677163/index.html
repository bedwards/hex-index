Just about all oneway streets are conversions of streets originally designed for two-way traffic. They were converted to move traffic faster. We can change these streets back to their original state. Even though they were designed to move traffic faster, they don't always do that.

Collisions go up and the severity is worse due to the higher speeds. A couple of studies have shown that one-way streets can depress areas economically, lowering property values and increasing crime.

Converting them back to two-way streets can have the opposite effect.
