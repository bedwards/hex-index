Prohibition

1 min read

Based on Wikipedia: Prohibition

In 1920, the United States Amendments XVIII and National Prohibition act of 1919 as part of the war emergency against the consumption of alcohol which was widely regarded as a menace to the working class made it illegal to drink in public. Yet by 1933, the federal government had reversed course, and the era of prohibition came to an end.
