German Idealism and the Problems of Neuropsychoanalysis

By Rafael Holmberg

I was recently invited to speak with Rahul Samaranayake on his podcast. As always, Rahul asked a series of intriguing as well as intense questions, which made for a truly enjoyable discussion.

We covered topics including German Idealism, neuroscience and psychoanalysis, phenomenology, and the reasons I have for believing that ‘neuropsychoanalysis’ is heading down a dark path. I hope you all enjoy the video, which is included below, and I can sincerely recommend following Rahul both on Substack and on YouTube.
