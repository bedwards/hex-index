On to Pushkin…

One of my reservations about “[The Coffin Maker](https://georgesaunders.substack.com/p/a-new-story-651)” (and many of you mentioned this as well) is that “it was all a dream” ending. The whole story, any story, is a dream of sorts – it didn’t happen and yet, while we’re in it, we’re asked to believe in it, and we agree to do so – and, in the case of a story with this sort of ending, our trust is, in a sense, violated.
