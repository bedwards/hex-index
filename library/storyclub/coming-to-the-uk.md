I want, also, to wish my wife, Paula, a very *happy birthday* today (November 13th) and let you all know about two upcoming events in support of her beautiful novel, *[Starting From Here.](https://bookshop.org/p/books/starting-from-here-a-novel-paula-saunders/7c4b8c360d3cc8e2?ean=9780593978290&next=t&)*

On Tuesday, November 18th, she’ll be appearing [here](https://rmwritersfest.org/writers-series/paula-saunders-starting-from-here-a-novel), in the Rancho Mirage Writers Series, interviewed by the wonderful Dr. Khoi Le…

…and on Monday, December 1st, she’ll be [here](https://godmothers.com/events/4040120251201), at Godmothers Books in Santa Barbara, interviewed by our dear friend and legendary photographer [Stewart Shining](https://www.stewartshining.com/) - it should be a very special conversation, as Stewart and Paula were next-door neighbors, growing up in Rapid City, South Dakota.

I’ll be in attendance at both of these events - in addition to being the love of my life, she’s a phenomenally inspiring speaker.

Beyond the (very low!) paywall, we’ll be starting a new story on Sunday - quite an old story, and very odd.

We’re off to New York this week, for the [National Book Awards ceremony](https://www.nationalbook.org/awards2025/awards-2025-lifetime/), where I’ll be receiving the Medal for Distinguished Contribution to American Letters, presented by my dear pal Deborah Treisman of *The New Yorker*. Should be a fun night and the whole process has evoked a lot of nostalgia and gratitude on my part, of course. More about that once I’m back…

And speaking of Deborah Treisman, here’s a [link](https://www.newyorker.com/culture/new-yorker-festival/george-saunders-and-zadie-smith-talk-with-deborah-treisman) to my event with Deborah and the amazing Zadie Smith at The New Yorker Fest a couple of weeks back.
