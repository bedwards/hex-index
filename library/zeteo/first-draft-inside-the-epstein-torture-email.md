# Tweeting Through the PedoFiles

[

![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!bzvL!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fce5a5c63-0b3b-439c-a64c-2141f0684233_1238x982.jpeg)



](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!bzvL!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fce5a5c63-0b3b-439c-a64c-2141f0684233_1238x982.jpeg)
**Jeffrey Epstein. Photo by Rick Friedman/Corbis via Getty Images.**

Amid its confused efforts to protect at least six powerful men associated with Jeffrey Epstein, the Department of Justice may have just revealed the hidden identity of someone involved in one of the more disturbing exchanges revealed from the Epstein files. We say ‘may’ because the Trump administration flatly refuses to give Americans real transparency.

This week, after repeated insistence from Congress, the DOJ allowed lawmakers to see the Epstein files, unredacted.

Except, as **Rep. Ro Khanna** [told me](https://zeteo.com/p/ro-khanna-reacts-epstein-files-new-redaction), much of the important information was, curiously, still redacted.

Khanna said Monday night that some such redactions covered survivor statements **“implicating rich and powerful men who raped these underage girls.”**

**“And that’s the information that the American people want to know: who are these powerful people who committed these heinous acts? Who were these people who showed up on Epstein’s island? And the fact is that they are being protected, and it’s disgusting,”** Khanna added.

He noted that some of those redactions **“protected”** six specific individuals – **“prominent”** men from both the US and other nations.

But it appears that as the DOJ defended its stonewalling, it may have walked into a rake, perhaps accidentally [revealing the identity](https://x.com/RepThomasMassie/status/2021059471804596548?s=20) of an individual whose identity was previously redacted. Or were they playing games?

On Monday night, Khanna’s colleague, **Thomas Massie**, took to Twitter and [wrote](https://x.com/RepThomasMassie/status/2020997755695468890?s=20), “**A Sultan seems to have sent this. DOJ should make this public.”** He attached an email exchange between Epstein and a redacted recipient, in which Epstein wrote, **“I loved the torture video,”** and the recipient replied: **“I am in china I will be in the US 2nd week of may.”**

Deputy Attorney General **Todd Blanche** [responded](https://x.com/dagtoddblanche/status/2021039636831781134?s=46), **“…you know that the Sultan’s name is available unredacted in the files. See EFTA00666117,”** presenting this as some sort of own. **“Be honest, and stop grandstanding.”**

That other [file](https://www.justice.gov/epstein/files/DataSet%209/EFTA00666117.pdf), EFTA00666117, features the name of [Sultan Ahmed bin Sulayem](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/features/2026-02-10/epstein-emails-show-dp-world-chairman-bin-sulayem-discussed-sex-business) – the chief of Dubai-based global logistics empire DP World, and the chairman of Dubai’s Ports, Customs & Free Zone Corporation.

Whether intentional or not, Blanche gave us the [near](https://x.com/RepThomasMassie/status/2021059471804596548?s=20) feeling of transparency regarding a disturbing email – but it very well could have just been out of an instinct coursing through this administration’s veins: when in doubt, tweet and attack.

The DOJ did not immediately respond to **Zeteo’s** request for clarification about what Blanche meant.

The possible horror abounds far beyond that.

Nine years old. That’s how young victims of Jeffrey Epstein may have been, [says](https://x.com/ChadPergram/status/2020943006107586662?s=20) Rep. **Jamie Raskin**.

The top Democrat on the House Judiciary Committee shared Khanna’s concern of many **“suspicious and baffling”** redactions in the documents, [including one related to](https://www.theguardian.com/us-news/2026/feb/09/jamie-raskin-doj-cover-up-epstein-files) billionaire businessman and Epstein [client Les Wexner](https://x.com/DAGToddBlanche/status/2021043176350355731?s=20).

**“What I saw today was that there were lots of examples of people’s names being redacted when they were not victims,”** Raskin [said](https://www.nbcnews.com/politics/trump-administration/live-blog/trump-congress-dhs-ice-immigration-epstein-files-doj-live-updates-rcna257949), noting that [Wexner’s](https://x.com/shadowofezra/status/2021057935149850806?s=46&t=F-u914q8nK6dJmjHHvnlIw) name was among them. He said he didn’t know whether the redactions were the result of **“sloppiness and negligence”** or something else, adding that Trump’s name was also redacted **“in a number of different places.”**

Also Monday, Epstein’s partner-in-crime, Ghislaine Maxwell, [declined](https://www.wsj.com/us-news/ghislaine-maxwell-takes-the-fifth-in-congressional-hearing-611f856e?mod=hp_listb_pos2) to answer lawmakers’ questions in a closed-door congressional deposition. Her lawyer said she would testify only if Trump – a longtime friend of Epstein’s – granted her clemency.

**“Only she can provide the complete account. Some may not like what they hear, but the truth matters,”** said her lawyer, David Oscar Markus. **“For example, both President Trump and President Clinton are innocent of any wrongdoing. Ms. Maxwell alone can explain why, and the public is entitled to that explanation.”**

The refusal infuriated some lawmakers. **“That’s simply her hiding information,”** Khanna said. **“And she’s using it as a bargaining chip. She’s saying, ‘If you give me clemency, I’ll spill the beans. I’ll tell you who these people were who raped young girls on Epstein’s Island.’ I said she needs to immediately be sent back to a maximum security prison.”**

(You can listen to Khanna reveal to me what it was like to view the secret unredacted Epstein files [here](https://zeteo.com/p/ro-khanna-reacts-epstein-files-new-redaction)).

The Trump-Vance administration opened its term by hosting a faux Epstein file release party, complete with [binders](https://www.cbsnews.com/news/right-wing-influencers-get-binders-labeled-the-epstein-files-but-downplay-revelations/) to be toted around by smiling right-wing influencers. It continued by oscillating between proclaiming itself to be the savior of victims…and calling the whole thing a [hoax](https://www.c-span.org/clip/white-house-event/president-trump-calls-epstein-files-a-hoax/5168819). It then fought tooth and nail to prevent the release of the actual files, even [targeting](https://www.dailymail.co.uk/news/article-15293265/Heartbroken-MTG-savages-Trump-Esptein-text.html) some of its own MAGA loyalist rank-and-file. And now, with these redactions, it continues to try haphazardly covering up whatever it can, while also incompetently tripping over itself online.

Instead of transparency, more than one year in, we’re left deciphering tweets. About a *torture video*.

## **🗞️** What You Need to Know

-   **Prosecuting enemies:** The DOJ is [seeking](https://x.com/joshgerstein/status/2021021011269800264) to revive its cases against former FBI director James Comey and New York Attorney General Letitia James, and is urging the judiciary to greenlight the idea that Trump can appoint interim US attorneys to successive 120-day periods so they can sidestep Senate confirmation.
    
-   **Tariff takedown:** House Republicans are attempting to [revive](https://x.com/jakesherman/status/2021005577715913206?s=46) their ban on resolutions countering Trump’s tariff policies after the previous one expired last month. It comes in response to House Democrats [gearing up](https://www.politico.com/live-updates/2026/02/09/congress/house-vote-canada-tariffs-00772224) to challenge Trump’s tariffs, attempting to force a vote on a resolution tomorrow that would knock down his tariff on Canada.
    
-   **Scot-free Steve**: The Justice Department is seeking to [dismiss](https://www.politico.com/news/2026/02/09/steve-bannon-conviction-jan-6-committee-00771714) the indictment against far-right former Trump adviser Steve Bannon for defying a congressional subpoena to testify about Jan. 6. The move is being carried out by former Fox host and now US Attorney Jeanine Pirro and could effectively erase his conviction.
    
-   **Masks stay on in California**: A federal judge [ruled](https://storage.courtlistener.com/recap/gov.uscourts.cacd.995805/gov.uscourts.cacd.995805.63.0_29.pdf) that California may not prohibit federal officers from wearing masks under the new “No Secret Police Act” – that is, unless the state requires *all* law enforcement to de-mask. In the same decision, the judge declined to block California’s other ICE accountability bill, which requires officers to display name and badge numbers.
    
-   **Rümeysa overcomes Trump attacks:** An immigration judge [barred](https://www.reuters.com/legal/government/immigration-judge-rejects-trump-effort-deport-pro-palestinian-tufts-student-2026-02-09/) the Trump administration from deporting Tufts University student Rümeysa Öztürk. Last March, Öztürk was abducted and detained by masked ICE agents for writing an op-ed criticizing Israel, as part of the administration’s crackdown on foreign students for their pro-Palestine speech.
    
-   **War on climate:** The *Wall Street Journal* [reports](https://www.wsj.com/politics/policy/trump-to-repeal-landmark-climate-finding-in-huge-regulatory-rollback-ff7d58db?mod=breakingnews) that the Trump administration is planning this week to reverse the Obama administration’s “endangerment” finding, which determined that six greenhouse gases pose a danger to public health and welfare and is “the legal basis for federal greenhouse-gas regulation.”

“Israel is committing war crimes before our eyes. Their government talks the language of ethnic cleansing… This is rogue state behaviour. Let them pay the price as pariahs, with sanctions applied to the state, not just a few ministers.”

This is what UK Health Secretary Wes Streeting, and the UK’s [possible next prime minister](https://uk.news.yahoo.com/odds-next-prime-minister-keir-starmer-perter-mandelson-epstein-182711101.html?guccounter=1&guce_referrer=aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cuZ29vZ2xlLmNvbS8&guce_referrer_sig=AQAAANBaEwODr9mGWteHiBfbg3rPiQ1j2_QPgdt0UQFaxw8KKDQRZM56ve7TX_CoBsuRE2eNn-g0UBmdTiy5jQJ2UBzsnGPSPvqN7qULlHLH9uEu_X2sXXmrEhjpFwZJCQohJdK593iWZmyO9j3PGS2FNFktTqBMnnwJo2xdowsjsTBx), was saying in private to now-disgraced UK Ambassador to the US Peter Mandelson – whose name appears [all over](https://zeteo.com/p/peter-mandelson-epstein-secrets-leak-brown-uk) the latest tranche of the Epstein files.

Streeting [released his messages](https://news.sky.com/story/read-wes-streetings-messages-with-mandelson-in-full-13505439) in an attempt to stamp down speculation about his relationship with Mandelson, who was fired last fall after files revealed his continued association with Epstein. This week, Mandelson resigned from the Labour Party and the House of Lords, after the release of additional files revealed he allegedly received payments from Epstein and passed sensitive government information to him.

Streeting’s comments – while made privately – are the strongest rebuke of Israel’s genocide in Gaza so far by a sitting British cabinet minister.

The remarkable move by the Health secretary to release his messages, perhaps betrays how keen he is to generate pressure on Keir Starmer – who controversially appointed Mandelson as ambassador and is now under pressure to resign – and to garner support for his own rise to become the next prime minister instead.

It is worth noting that Streeting also notes in these private texts with the former UK ambassador to the US that he has “never been a shrinking violet on Israel,” adding that he has supported Labour Friends of Israel (LFI) for over 20 years.
