Okay, now that we’ve moved past our first holiday hurdle of 2024, please join me and comedian [Jake Cornell](https://www.instagram.com/jakewcornell/?hl=en) to navigate the chaos of holiday parties, hosting challenges, gift dilemmas, and general December stressors. Jake and I bonded over many things: being children of divorce, our pathological need to BYOB (bring your own bulbs) to Airbnbs, and our love for two very specific non-alcoholic beveraginos to keep the night going while avoiding hangovers (or, if you’re simply not drinking).

**On this week's episode of Solicited Advice:**

-   **AITA?** How do I gracefully tell a friend in their much too small with not enough seats apartment (...in Murray Hill) they should let other people host once in a while?
    
-   **HOSTING AND DRINKING = BAD?** I love drinking while hosting, but I struggle with finding my limit. How do I avoid hangovers while also enjoying myself \*in the moment\*?
    
-   **WHAT TO DRINK WHEN YOU’RE NOT DRINKING:** Favorite non-alcoholic drinks this holiday season (or any season)?
    
-   **GIFT-RELATED ANXIETY:** What’s the best way to handle gifts I don’t like? And as someone whose love language is giving thoughtful gifts, how do I cope when others don’t do the same?
    
-   **HOLIDAY COOKING / CLEANING ANXIETY:** How can I keep things clean while I cook? How tidy does my space need to be when guests are over? And is it better to clean up at night or leave it for the morning?
