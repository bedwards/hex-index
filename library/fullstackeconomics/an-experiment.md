My goal with this newsletter is to not only write about the economy as it exists today, but also to explore how technology could change the economy in the years to come. I’ve written about [delivery robots](https://www.fullstackeconomics.com/p/the-robot-takeout-revolution-is-closer), [falling battery costs](https://www.fullstackeconomics.com/p/untitled-2), and [Amazon’s Just Walk Out technology](https://www.fullstackeconomics.com/p/i-visited-the-first-whole-foods-that-lets-you-skip-the-checkout-line).

In recent weeks I’ve been thinking the most about generative AI software like ChatGPT and Stable Diffusion. These technologies have advanced rapidly and I expect them to have large and unpredictable effects on the economy in the coming years.

I want to spend the next few months reporting on these technologies, the companies behind them, and their economic and policy implications.
