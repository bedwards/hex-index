Here’s Yakima again:

Rizz’s website says it serves Northampton, Lehigh, and Monroe counties in PA and Warren and Hunterdon counties in NJ. Should be easy from there, right? Here’s how my fiendishly clever chain of reasoning led me astray:

  1. Three cars in the view have front license plates. New Jersey requires them, Pennsylvania does not. Thus we’re in New Jersey. (Wrong!)

  2. A Google search for “Rizz containers” now autocompletes “Rizz containers and front license plates,” so at least one other sleuth is already on the same scent.

  3. Sharp low-angle light on the background trees and horizontal shadow borders on dormers suggests it’s an early morning view with light from the east, so we’re looking north. (Wrong!)

  4. Shouldn’t be too hard to do a search for the steeple in Warren and Hunterdon counties. Came up empty!

  5. Probably from a hotel, so look at all of those in Warren and Hunterdon counties. None have an adjacent matching corner.

  6. Sidewalks, parking signs, and lack of front yards say we’re urbanish. We’re looking at a row of colonial-style houses on the interior of a sharp, obtuse-angled street corner, with a distinctive wedge-shaped corner yard. The western NJ road system, while oriented parallel to Appalachian ridges instead of north-south, is 99% rectilinear. The obtuse corner I want should be easily recognizable on satellite view. Warren and Hunterdon counties aren’t that big. Can’t be too hard to do a brute force search in satellite view. Nada!

  7. Maybe Rizz’s website is out of date? I phoned them to see if they’ve expanded their NJ service area, and they said they don’t even do residential pickup in NJ, except for a few HOAs and property management groups. So... maybe it could be in PA after all?

  8. Repeat hotel search for Northampton, Lehigh, and Monroe counties in PA. No go.

  9. I tried a semi-random brute search of Northampton, Lehigh, and Monroe counties — but the area is much bigger and more densely populated than adjacent NJ. Gave up in discouragement.

  10. Finally, I repeated the steeple search.
