**Johnnyk2000** has a legendary reputation for unearthing scandalous information about the elected officials in Austin City Hall.

Johnnyk files dozens of public records information requests with the City of Austin every year.

With the uproar surrounding Zo Qadri's anti-Semitic tweets earlier this month, both Johnnyk2000 and former Austin City Council candidate **Clinton Rarey** (twitter: @[clintonrarey](https://twitter.com/clintonrarey)) filed public information requests to view copies of Mr. Qadri's emails.

These two brilliant, aggressive citizen journalists wanted to gain a better understanding of how Zo Qadri and his staff were dealing with the political fallout.

Either **look it up** in the dictionary, or **swap it out** for a word you understand.

Especially when that unfamiliar word could change the meaning of the sentence to the exact opposite of what you had intended.
