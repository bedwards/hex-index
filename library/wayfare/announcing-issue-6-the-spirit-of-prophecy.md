Friends,

We’re delighted to announce **Wayfare Issue 6.** This issue explores the spirit of prophecy and features:

-   Writing by Adam Miller, Terryl Givens, James Goldberg, and Hannah Crowther
    
-   Art by J. Kirk Richards, Brian Kershisnik, Kathleen Peterson, and Sarah Hawkes
    
-   Poetry by Elizabeth Garcia, Lance Larsen, and Doug Talley
    
-   Plus a comic by Camilla Stark, a beautiful new hymn, and a guide for pilgrims on the Camino de Santiago.
