The pace of Russian advances as well as the extended range of glide bombs and FPV drones forced Ukrainians to prepare fortifications several dozen kilometers from the current front line. The trenches for soldiers themselves have also been prepared based on current battlefield experience.

Instead of trench lines stretching for dozens or hundreds of meters, many independent smaller strong points are prepared, which means that the fall of one of them does not lead to the collapse of defense on a section and is supposed to force the Russians to capture each strong point separately. The defensive positions themselves are built along the forest plantings or in groves which provides cover from drone attacks and significantly complicates their potential detection.
