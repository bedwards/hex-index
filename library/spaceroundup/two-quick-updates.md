Dear readers!

I wanted to share a couple of updates with you.

After a looong two-year pause following the birth of my daughter, I have returned to writing The Space Roundup, but now as part of The Space Race team. I am sending it every Tuesday. If you would like to keep up with the latest space updates, you can subscribe here: **[https://www.thespacerace.news/subscribe](https://www.thespacerace.news/subscribe)**

**Wait, there’s more!**  
Additionally, I have been working on a non-space-related project that aligns with my passion for sharing a positive and hopeful perspective on reality.

The media and social networks often amplify negative news to gain more clicks and impressions, which leads to sadness, anxiety, and a negative view of the world. This affected me too!

That's why I decided to launch **["Latest Good News"](http://latestgoodnews.com/)** a system that gathers and analyzes hundreds of headlines daily to select the most positive, constructive, and hopeful ones. These are then sent out as a newsletter to a community of subscribers.

Since creating it, initially for myself and then for friends and family, I start each day with a positive message, helping me focus better and being happier and more relaxed.

If you're curious and would like to try it out, you can sign up for free here:

**[http://latestgoodnews.com/](http://latestgoodnews.com/)**

Looking forward to hearing back from you!

Cheers,
