There is a reason I regularly start my posts with the following sentence: *“One of the most common questions I get is…“*

My posts are often inspired by your questions. When I am asked something repeatedly, I know that something interesting is there. An intuitive explanation. An unexpected story.

However, I have never written about the actual most common question:

> *“How do you make your illustrations?”*

You ask this under almost all of my posts, and I am happy to answer every time.
