Hey friends,

This is Isaac Saul from [Tangle](https://www.readtangle.com/).

I’m just writing with an important note to let you know that we are no longer on Substack. In the last few months, we’ve seen a number of people sign up for our content here on Substack (presumably, because you Google’d us and found a link to our Substack there).

But we actually moved off of Substack a few years ago. That’s nothing against Substack: We love this platform, the founders, and the network. It was a pure business decision.

**Still, if you want to keep up with our work, you need to go to [readtangle.com](https://www.readtangle.com/) to find us.**

I’ve tried exporting as many subscribers as possible from Substack over to our Tangle website — so you may already be getting our emails from there. But if not, this is just a courtesy heads up that you’ll find our content [over on that website now](https://www.readtangle.com/).

Thank you!

Best,

Isaac & the Tangle team
