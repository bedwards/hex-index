#### Three New Kids on The Block Worth Checking Out!

1.  **The \- Brainchild of & . Here is their [website.](https://flashfictioninstitute.com/) Some wonderful offerings available. I am most honored to be a member of the Advisory Board for this worthy endeavor.**
    

> *“The Flash Fiction Institute is a space for writers of micro-fiction, tiny stories, short-shorts, miniatures, tiny fiction, sudden fiction, hint fiction, postcard fiction, post-it fiction, prose poetry, and all those curious about how less is more. In short, the Flash Fiction Institute is an online school and resource hub for Flash Fiction writers.”*

2.  **[Lit Match Collective](https://www.litmatchcollective.com/)** \- Founded by (fiction editor of *Okay Donkey*) and **Autumn Eve Watts** (fiction editor of *Guernica*) and launched September 1st, LMC is a community space for writers to connect and grow with LOTS of offerings and three membership levels. **I’ll be posting fun prompts and challenges on their Discord channel on the 1st and 15th of each month, so check them out!**
    

> *“Our scheduled activities, events, and discussions offer company and coaching throughout the creative process. From [drafting](https://www.litmatchcollective.com/accountability-sessions) and [development](https://www.litmatchcollective.com/process-groups), [revision](https://www.litmatchcollective.com/writing-workshops) and [more revision](https://www.litmatchcollective.com/drop-in-coaching), to [polish](https://www.litmatchcollective.com/flexible-feedback) and [submission](https://www.litmatchcollective.com/custom-lists): we're here to help. We host communal [writing sessions](https://www.litmatchcollective.com/accountability-sessions) to get you working 💪, live [process groups](https://www.litmatchcollective.com/process-groups) to stay on track 🎯, member [readings](https://www.litmatchcollective.com/firelight-readarounds) to build confidence 🔥, and community [spaces](https://www.litmatchcollective.com/campground-commons) to discuss craft, publishing, and writing life ✨ ...plus a whole lot [more](https://www.litmatchcollective.com/special-events)! (including corny jokes).”*

3.  ​**[Hot Flash Literary](https://hotflashliterary.com/) - Founded by editors and , they’ve just put out their stunning [inaugural issue,](https://hotflashliterary.com/current-issue/) including words by Mikki Arnoff, Christine H. Chen, Diane Gottlieb, Stella Reed, Chelsea Stickle, Nancy Stohlman, Huina Zheng, and yours truly! Consider sending them something for Issue #2!**
    

> *“Hot Flash Literary is an online journal of flash whose mission is to give women-identifying writers a voice; specifically, it’s a place for women’s voices to ignite. Like an ember.*
> 
> *Issues are loosely themed, with a goal of highlighting the experience of being a woman, whatever that means to each individual writer. The themes are meant to inspire but not constrain.”*
