Christine Swedowsky, the newest publisher of [World Editions](https://worldeditions.org/), and Michelle Mirabella, translator of Catalina Infante’s *[The Cracks We Bear](https://bookshop.org/a/164/9781642861594)* join Chad to discuss the various eras of World Editions and how an indie press can evolve over time, and a few upcoming titles. The conversation focuses on Mirabella & Infante’s forthcoming novel, discussing how the book is much more than another book about motherhood, the difficulty of translating certain titles for the American market, and how valuable a collaborative experience with your publisher can be.

This week’s music is “Burn This Atlas Down (2 of Clubs)” from Hallelujah the Hills.
