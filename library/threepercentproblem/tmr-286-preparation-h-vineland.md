Peter Coviello (*[Vineland Reread](https://bookshop.org/a/164/9780231185219)*) joins Kaija, Brian, and Chad to talk about the ending of *[Vineland](https://bookshop.org/a/164/9780141180632)*, how it compares to *One Battle After Another*, what the Thanatoids are, the sheer joy of reading Thomas Pynchon, and more. A very lively way to end the season filled with laughs and Coviello’s wonderful insights.

Up next, for Season 29 is . . . you’ll have to wait and see. Announcement coming soon, but in the meantime, be on the lookout for a one-off episode about Pynchon’s new book, *[Shadow Ticket](https://bookshop.org/a/164/9781594206108).*

This week’s music is “Maybelline” by Chuck Berry.
