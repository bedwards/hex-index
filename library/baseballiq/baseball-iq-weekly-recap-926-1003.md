Say what you will about whether the expanded playoffs are good for the league in the long run\*, but the sheer quantity of playoff baseball this past week has been a shock to my system in a very good way. It feels like a reward for the late start and all those months where sports should have been on TV, but weren’t.

\*My personal take is that it’s too friendly toward below-average teams. There’s no reason a team like this year’s Brewers should be rewarded for a season where they never once played above .500.
