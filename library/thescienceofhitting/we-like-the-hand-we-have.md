On Thursday, [Versant (VSNT)](https://www.cnbc.com/2025/05/06/comcast-spinco-corporate-name-versant.html) published its [preliminary information statement](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/2067876/000119312525207527/d894612dex991.htm#tx894612_8) in advance of its spin-off from Comcast (CMCSA). The decision to pursue this separation, announced [in November 2024](https://www.wsj.com/business/media/comcast-greenlights-7-billion-spinoff-of-nbcuniversal-cable-channels-cc5c6dc5), comes at an interesting moment for the media industry. Most notably, Warner Bros. Discovery (WBD) [was also preparing](https://www.wbd.com/news/warner-bros-discovery-separate-two-leading-media-companies) to spin its global linear channel assets – a move now up in the air [as Paramount Skydance (PSKY) reportedly](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=j5p8k8ossYo) intends to bid for *[all](https://www.wsj.com/business/media/paramount-skydance-prepares-ellison-backed-bid-for-warner-bros-discovery-0b921c20)* of WBD.
