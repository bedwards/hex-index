With certain companies, you recognize early in the research process that you’ve found something unique. It’s evident in the way management talks about their business, in the way they talk about strategic decisions, in the strength of their financial results, and, invariably, in the long-term stock price chart. There’s a sense that the company is different in some notable ways.

Kinsale seems to fit that description to a tee.
