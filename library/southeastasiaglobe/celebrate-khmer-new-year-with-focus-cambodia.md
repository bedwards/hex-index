> Hello *Globe* readers,
> 
> We’re excited to announce that our sister publication, *[Focus Cambodia](https://focus-cambodia.com/),* has recently released the 2024/25 print edition of the magazine.
> 
> This 150 plus page book is released once a year and is meant to be the ultimate guide to business, lifestyle and culture in Cambodia. In its pages you’ll find exclusive interviews with some of the Kingdom’s newest government representatives, leading business people and most popular celebrities. In the guide section you can read the team’s recommendations for where to sleep, eat, drink and explore, when in the country.
> 
> Print copies will be delivered following the Khmer New Year, so please connect if you would like to arrange your copy today. You can do so by replying to this email.
> 
> In the meantime check out the [digital version of the magazine here](https://bit.ly/3UdEPN8).
> 
> Don’t forget that you can [subscribe to the](https://focuscambodia.substack.com/?r=2p43rj&utm_campaign=pub&utm_medium=web) *[Focus](https://focuscambodia.substack.com/?r=2p43rj&utm_campaign=pub&utm_medium=web)* [newsletter](https://focuscambodia.substack.com/?r=2p43rj&utm_campaign=pub&utm_medium=web) to stay informed of all things Cambodia.
> 
> Happy Khmer New Year!
> 
> *Southeast Asia Globe*
