> Hello *Globe* readers,
> 
> We’re checking in to share an update about *[Focus Cambodia](https://focus-cambodia.com/)*, our sister publication and the ultimate guide to the Kingdom of Wonder.
> 
> While the team at *Focus* is already publishing regularly online, they’re also working hard on a new print edition of the magazine, which will be released early next year. Featuring business leaders, policy makers and artists, the magazine will give readers an inside look into the people, culture and destinations that make up modern Cambodia.
> 
> A premium glossy-covered publication with more than 160 pages full of beautiful photography, exclusive interviews and original features, *Focus Cambodia* will distribute 30,000 copies across the Kingdom. It is currently offering an “Early Bird” discount to advertisers throughout November.
> 
> If you would like to learn more about *Focus Cambodia* and receive the “Early Bird” rates simply reply to this email to get in touch.
> 
> Also, don’t forget that you can [subscribe to the](https://focuscambodia.substack.com/?r=2p43rj&utm_campaign=pub&utm_medium=web) *[Focus Cambodia](https://focuscambodia.substack.com/?r=2p43rj&utm_campaign=pub&utm_medium=web)* [newsletter](https://focuscambodia.substack.com/?r=2p43rj&utm_campaign=pub&utm_medium=web) to stay informed of all things Cambodia.
>
