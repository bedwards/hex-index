#### [Cambodian monkey exports to Canada for lab tests are surging, fueling health concerns](https://southeastasiaglobe.com/cambodian-monkey-trade-with-canada/)

> Hello readers,
> 
> We know it’s been a few months since you’ve heard from us, but *Southeast Asia Globe* had one last dance with our [three-years-long investigative series](https://southeastasiaglobe.com/small-monkeys-are-big-business-in-cambodias-animal-trade/) into Cambodia’s murky monkey business.
> 
> In collaboration with *[The Toronto Star](https://www.thestar.com/news/investigations/a-company-stopped-importing-monkeys-to-the-u-s-for-lab-tests-over-smuggling-concerns/article_81a108ca-e61a-11ee-8c87-07c937fe93d9.html)* and backed by *[The Pulitzer Center](https://pulitzercenter.org/)*’s *[Rainforest Investigations Network](https://pulitzercenter.org/journalism/initiatives/rainforest-investigations-network-initiative)*, *Globe* dug deep into how [Cambodia’s exports of endangered long-tailed macaques](https://southeastasiaglobe.com/cambodias-monkey-farms-corruption-taints-wildlife-trade/) had pivoted from the U.S. to Canada.
> 
> After a [2022 arrest and indictment](https://southeastasiaglobe.com/cambodian-officials-charged-with-wildlife-trafficking-a-wake-up-call-for-global-monkey-trade/), Cambodia’s direct exports to laboratories in the U.S. slowed, but *Globe* uncovered an uptick to its northern neighbor, Canada.
