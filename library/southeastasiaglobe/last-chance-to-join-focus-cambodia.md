> Hello *Globe* readers,
> 
> Welcome to December. The last month of the year and the final chance to book your ad in *[Focus Cambodia](https://focus-cambodia.com/)* 2024.
> 
> *Focus* is the sister publication of *Globe*, covering business, lifestyle and culture in Cambodia. Once a year, *Focus* publishes a print magazine with more than 160 pages of beautiful photography, exclusive interviews and original features. With 30,000 copies distributed across the Kingdom, *Focus Cambodia* is a premium channel for businesses to place their brands and share their thought leadership and insights.
> 
> If you would like to learn more about advertising in *Focus Cambodia* before it is too late, simply reply to this email to get in touch.
> 
> Also, don’t forget that you can [subscribe to the](https://focuscambodia.substack.com/?r=2p43rj&utm_campaign=pub&utm_medium=web) *[Focus](https://focuscambodia.substack.com/?r=2p43rj&utm_campaign=pub&utm_medium=web)* [newsletter](https://focuscambodia.substack.com/?r=2p43rj&utm_campaign=pub&utm_medium=web) to stay informed of all things Cambodia.
> 
> Best thoughts,
