### Howdy cookbook fans!

And hello from chilly Austin. Apologies for this being a smidge late, this past week has been rather intense for me on a personal level and sometimes real life takes priority.

But the cookbooks keep on coming, and where there are fall cookbook releases, that’s where I’ll be, too. Things are starting to slow down a bit as we get closer to the holidays (maybe that’s why [September](https://www.stainedpagenews.com/p/introducing-the-cookbooks-of-september) and [October](https://www.stainedpagenews.com/p/introducing-the-cookbooks-of-october) were so nuts), but there are still some gems in this batch. Also! Since the list is short we can do allll the covers, for your viewing enjoyment. Here we go!

-   YouTuber ([1m subscribers](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCjBaM8gCNWKXDqU5g8mTNKw)) and Instagrammer ([248k subscribers](https://www.instagram.com/thedannirose/?hl=en)) **Danni Rose** has written her first cookbook: ***[Danni’s Juke Joint Comfort Food Cookbook](https://bookshop.org/a/3497/9780063281059)***, which is full of her signature recipes from her Southern family, including dishes served at her dad’s titular juke joint. Recipes include dishes like “Cheddar Pancakes with BBQ Pulled Pork, Daddy's Fried Cheese Grits, Cajun Deviled Eggs, Jack & Pepsi Slushies, Spatchcocked Whole Fried Chicken, Ole Skool Mac 'n' Cheese, Pot Likker Greens, \[and\] Church Lady Candied Apples.” *Harvest, November 7.*

Instead, you’ll get cookies, bars, brownies, loaves, and, you know, a few cakes. Cakes are great! *Clarkson Potter, November 7.*

-   Author of 2021’s bestseller *[Jubilee](https://bookshop.org/a/3497/9781524761738)* **Toni Tipton-Martin** turns her eye to cocktails in ***[Juke Joints, Jazz Clubs, and Juice: Cocktails from Two Centuries of African American Cookbooks](https://bookshop.org/a/3497/9780593233825)***. The book digs into the history of classic cocktails and brings readers to the present with modern renditions. *Clarkson Potter, November 13.*

-   Los Angeles chef and cookbook author **Nancy Silverton** returns with a book that seeks to perfect the American baking cannon: ***[The Cookie That Changed My Life](https://bookshop.org/a/3497/9780593321669)***. Per the publisher’s copy, “Biting into a particularly delicious peanut butter cookie one day, she and had an epiphany: *every single thing we bake should taste this good*,” and thus she set off to improve classics like apple pie, carrot cake, cornbread, biscuits, lemon bars, and much more. Written with **Carolynn Carreño**. *Knopf, November 14.*

-   **Snoop Dogg** is back with a new cookbook, and this time he’s brought a friend: in ***[Snoop Dogg Presents Goon with the Spoon](https://bookshop.org/a/3497/9781797213712)***, he collaborates with pal/rapper **Earl “E-40” Stevens**, the titular “goon with the spoon.” The recipes are a collection of dishes the pals like to cook together as well as some recipes from E-40’s Filipino food brand, [The Lumpia Company](https://www.instagram.com/thelumpiacompany/?hl=en). *Chronicle, November 14.*

Way’s thing is he makes accessible restaurant-quality food; recipes include PB&J Brioche French Toast, Herby Breadcrumb Salmon Traybake, Gnocchi Carbonara, Pork Ribs with Cucumber Slaw, Smash Burgers, Korean Fried Popcorn Chicken, Chicory, Blue Cheese & Grapefruit Salad, and Strawberry Cheesecake Ice Cream. *Voracious, November 14.*
