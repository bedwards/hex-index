5.  One of my favorite publishers, [Drawn and Quarterly](https://drawnandquarterly.com), is having a holiday [flash sale](https://mailchi.mp/drawnandquarterly/2025-holiday-flash-sale-14864920?e=f30a5e1f6f) this weekend. 6.  New gear in the studio: I spent a lot of this year experimenting with color, starting with [block printing](https://austinkleon.substack.com/p/an-unofficial-guide-to-block-printing) (Speedball makes an easy [beginner block printing kit](https://geni.us/QLQB3)) and continuing with [drawing succulents](https://austinkleon.substack.com/p/for-no-good-reason) with [Caran d’Ache](https://geni.us/XlFUla) pastels. I use these [acrylic book stands](https://geni.us/DkMcE3) to prop up books, CDs, and cassettes. Meg got me [this cup cap accessory](https://geni.us/peQX6z) for my [Yeti Rambler](https://geni.us/5aP8r), so now it works great in all seasons.  
      
    
7.  Old favorites: I write and draw with [Pentel Portable Pocket Brush pens](https://geni.us/n73R), a [Lamy Safari fountain pen](https://geni.us/PMBGhZm), [Pilot G-2 Bolds](https://geni.us/AAMoPRg), [Pentel Sign Pens](https://geni.us/yyjA), and [Posca paint markers](https://geni.us/RZ2NYue). My favorite notebook is an [A6 pocket-sized Leuchtturm1917](https://www.leuchtturm1917.us/classic-notebooks-1.html). My very favorite pencil is still the [Musgrave 600 NEWS](https://musgravepencil.com/products/600-news-wood-cased-round-pencil-musgrave-pencil-company), which I make last with [Derwent pencil extenders](https://geni.us/bSpZaX). I love my [Trodat professional self-inking date stamp](https://geni.us/BJh3y4) and [their self-inking DIY stamp kits](https://geni.us/LTr0) for custom return addresses and other messages. Almost every day I use my [cutting mat](https://geni.us/VTcTBsT), a [set of X-Acto knives](https://geni.us/QhZoOB), [orange-handled Fiskar scissors](https://geni.us/xKqbb0i), a [bone folder](https://geni.us/Tc7yzIe), and [UHU glue sticks](https://geni.us/Y75SU). (Outside the studio, I still keep [a pad of Aqua Notes](https://geni.us/XfsnNYS) in the shower and [a Rite in the Rain spiral notebook](https://geni.us/Ej0CZz) beside the pool.)  
      
    
8.  Music gear I splurged on for our home studio: This [Focusrite audio interface](https://geni.us/e66X24) plugs right into an iPad and plays nice with GarageBand. I love this [Orange Crush 50W bass amp](https://geni.us/8cApuh). I wish I could put [this Line 6 looping pedal](https://geni.us/uYaNIFA) in a time machine and send it to my teenage self. The [Akai MPK Mini Play](https://geni.us/N83Asz) doubles as a good MIDI controller and standalone travel keyboard — we stuck it in one of the kids’ book bags and they played it a bunch on vacation. (I still think [Wonderboom bluetooth speakers](https://geni.us/yEUWeCi) are great for casual music listening inside and outside of the house.)  
      
    
9.  Kids’ stuff: My kids (10 & 13) love [Nathan Hale’s](https://geni.us/RF5SRR7) *[Hazardous Tales](https://geni.us/RF5SRR7)* [series](https://geni.us/RF5SRR7). On holiday breaks we love to solve a puzzle from the [Magic Puzzle Company](https://magicpuzzlecompany.com) together. We play UNO with a [regular deck](https://geni.us/2qSxP8), but if we didn’t, I might try to trick them into playing with [Super Mario](https://geni.us/A3HGI) or [Minecraft](https://geni.us/PSaH9T) decks. (I bought an [automatic card shuffler](https://geni.us/FlxvNhp) a while back and it’s good fun.) These [thermal printer cameras](https://geni.us/P6t7Ba) are pretty cool. I’m surprised how often they use the [Paper Mate triangular mechanical pencils](https://geni.us/lB7j3) and [sticky notes](https://geni.us/XLrd) we put in their stockings. They write in their [one line a day diaries](https://geni.us/jq0FkGu) every night with their these [retractable cat gel pens](https://geni.us/KSsQWLB). My youngest loves his [Carl Angel-5](https://geni.us/a2YmI) pencil sharpener. We buy them lots of paper books but they love the [Kindle Paperwhite for Kids](https://geni.us/AgC72). I wish I’d got them one of these [bug catchers](https://geni.us/KpoZ) when they were little. (Better late than never!)
