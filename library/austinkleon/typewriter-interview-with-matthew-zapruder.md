Hey y’all,

[Matthew Zapruder](https://matthewzapruder.com/about/) is the author of many books of poetry and books about poetry, including *[Why Poetry](https://geni.us/4mltVy1)* and *[Story of a Poem: A Memoir](https://geni.us/jMBq)*. This year he released the paperback edition of his latest book of poetry, *[I Love Hearing Your Dreams](https://geni.us/64Nr),* and a limited edition chapbook called *[How To Continue](https://matthewzapruder.substack.com/p/how-to-continue-the-opposite-of-ai).*

This [typewriter interview](https://austinkleon.substack.com/t/typewriter-interviews) was conducted via the magic of the United States Postal Service. (For a plain-text version with links, see the P.S. below.)
