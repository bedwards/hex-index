Hey y’all,

[Elizabeth McCracken](https://elizabethmccracken.substack.com) is the acclaimed and bestselling author of [nine books](https://elizabethmccracken.com/books/). A former public librarian, she has taught writing for over 35 years. To celebrate the release of her latest book, *[A Long Game: Notes on Writing Fiction](https://geni.us/crq6)*, she borrowed my Smith Corona GT Ghia and typed these answers while packing up her office in Austin, Texas to move with her family to England.

(For a plain-text version of this interview with links and notes, see the P.S. below.)
