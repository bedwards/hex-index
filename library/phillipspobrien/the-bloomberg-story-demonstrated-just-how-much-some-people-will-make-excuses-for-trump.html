“You got to say, look, they want this. You’ve got to convince him with this. You know, that’s a very standard form of negotiation. I haven’t heard it, but I heard it was standard negotiation, and I would imagine he’s saying the same thing to Ukraine, because each party has to give and take,”

So there we have it. Trump is more than happy with Witkoff’s behavior and has confirmed that Witkoff is his point-man for the Moscow talks. The shocking thing is that some people thought otherwise.
