#### **Ukraine Opens Up A New War At Sea**

Yesterday two separate reports were made of Ukrainian operations at sea; reports which combined reveal that the war at sea is poised to expand significantly (if that indeed is the Ukrainian wish). The two stories were:

-   [The Ukrainians attacked two of Russia’s shadow tanker fleet as they were passing near parts of the Turkish coast on the Black Sea](https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/articles/cp9ky5z2gx0o).
    
-   Even more surprising, the Ukrainians attacked [a Turkish owned tanker](https://www.tradewindsnews.com/tankers/tanker-linked-to-russia-trade-hit-by-explosions-off-west-africa/2-1-1909083) *[Mersin](https://www.tradewindsnews.com/tankers/tanker-linked-to-russia-trade-hit-by-explosions-off-west-africa/2-1-1909083)* [as it was carrying Russian oil off the coast of Senegal](https://www.tradewindsnews.com/tankers/tanker-linked-to-russia-trade-hit-by-explosions-off-west-africa/2-1-1909083). The vessel was reported as sinking, though the crew was evacuated.
