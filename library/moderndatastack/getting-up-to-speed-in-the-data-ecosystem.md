I recently joined [Atlan,](https://atlan.com/) a DataOps platform, and have been trying to get up to speed in the Data & Analytics stack over the past 30 days. While I’ve spent my entire career in Data & AI businesses, there is still so much to learn. I’ve read through countless blogs, market maps, analyst reports, product sheets, and “For Dummies” Guides.

What was most obvious throughout this process, though?

There’s no go-to newsletter on the Modern Data Stack.

I thought I’d share some of the best writing I found along the way. Here’s my attempt!

### **Nuggets from the ecosystem:**

[A topical Twitter debate on “the best analytics stack](https://twitter.com/lennysan/status/1331381900343083008)” [Favorite comment from Huy Nguyen, the Founder of Holistics.io, a data modeling platform:](https://twitter.com/iamhuy/status/1331407667709968385)

> *"Attributes of a good analytics stack:  
> \* ELT over ETL  
> \* Cloud DW over on-premise DW/Hadoop systems.  
> \* Data modeling is essential  
> \* SQL based over non-SQL based analytics.  
> \* Analytics workflow is more important than singular focus on visualizations"*
