# **“Eno” in the LA Times, POV, Indiewire, NY Times, BBC Radio, Variety, Screen Daily…**

[

![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!KlWQ!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fe5bc7161-2526-4b25-9f5c-7238294996fe_1500x1500.jpeg)



](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!KlWQ!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fe5bc7161-2526-4b25-9f5c-7238294996fe_1500x1500.jpeg)

**“Eno” has been shortlisted for the Academy Award for Best Documentary Feature,** and this has caused a flurry of press about the film. Read new pieces in the [LA Times](https://www.latimes.com/entertainment-arts/awards/story/2025-01-06/brian-eno-music-doc-unique-version-every-time) and [POV Magazine](https://povmagazine.com/how-eno-unlocks-the-creative-potential-for-documentary/), plus “Best of 2024” and Oscars inclusions in the [New York Times](https://www.nytimes.com/interactive/2025/01/05/movies/critics-oscar-nominees.html?referringSource=articleShare&smid=nytcore-ios-share), [Indiewire](https://www.indiewire.com/gallery/indiewire-staff-favorite-films-tv-shows-2024), [Variety,](https://variety.com/lists/best-documentaries-of-2024/eno-2/) and [Screen Daily](https://www.screendaily.com/features/films-of-the-year-2024-wendy-ide/5200363.article).

**Brian Eno** also talked about the film and his forthcoming book “What Art Does” this week on [BBC Radio’s Front Row](https://www.bbc.co.uk/sounds/play/m0026nk6) program. Brian’s bit starts at 29:18.
