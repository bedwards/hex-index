**Dieter Rams turns 92 today**, and it’s now a tradition that on Dieter’s birthday [I stream my documentary “Rams” free](https://www.ohyouprettythings.com/rams) to viewers worldwide. You may have seen it already, if so here’s a chance to revisit this film about one of the most influential designers on the planet, with an original soundtrack by some guy named Brian Eno. Forward to a friend who might be interested! The film is [free to stream](https://www.ohyouprettythings.com/rams) for the next 48 hours. Happy Birthday Dieter!

## **Midwest “Eno” Screenings This Week, Australia Next Week**

**We had such amazing events last week in San Francisco, Portland and Washington DC.** Thanks so much to everyone who attended. Detroit, Milwaukee, and Chicago: you’re up next! Join me this week for live screenings of “Eno” and post-film conversations. Then next week I head down under for a week of events in Australia: three days at the **Sydney Opera House** for the Vivid LIVE festival, and two days at **ACMI** in Melbourne.

We’ve also announced multi-night cinema runs of the film in July at **Film Forum** in NYC, **Vidiots** in LA, and the **Roxie** in San Francisco. Many more to come.

**May 21 - Detroit - Detroit Film Theater**  
Live screening event and post-film conversation with Gary Hustwit.  
Get tickets for the [7pm](https://www.eventbee.com/v/enodetroit#/tickets) show.

**May 22 - Milwaukee - Oriental Theatre**  
Live screening event and post-film conversation with Gary Hustwit. Co-presented with Milwaukee Film.  
Get tickets for the [7:30pm](https://mkefilm.org/oriental-theatre/events/eno) show.

**May 23 - Chicago - The Music Box**  
Live screening event and post-film conversation with Gary Hustwit and special guests. Co-presented with MurMur Ring.  
Get tickets for the [7pm](https://www.eventbee.com/v/eno-chicago-premiere-7pm/event?eid=215214074#/tickets) show. \[LOW TICKETS\]

**May 31 to June 2 - Sydney Opera House**  
The Australian Premiere of “Eno” as part of Vivid LIVE, with four live screening events presented by Gary Hustwit.  
[Get tickets.](https://www.sydneyoperahouse.com/vivid-live/film/eno)
