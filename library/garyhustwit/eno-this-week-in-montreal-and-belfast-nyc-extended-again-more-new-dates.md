**Howdy friends!** Film Forum in **NYC** has extended “Eno” for a 7th week! This week also marks the **Belfast** premiere of the film, and this Thursday I’ll be in **Montreal** for a live performance screening at the MUTEK Festival. And by popular demand “Eno” is coming back to the Roxie in **San Francisco** for another week! Looking ahead to September, we’ve got the **Prague** and **Dublin** premieres, encore **Nashville** screenings, a screening at the **Camden International Film Festival**, a night of live screenings at the ICA in **Boston**, and much more.

# **Now Streaming: Four Bonus EP’s of Music from the Film**

[

![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!k5Gm!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F83c99de5-67fe-4b25-b9ea-7fa958b91eb1_1432x1491.jpeg)



](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!k5Gm!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F83c99de5-67fe-4b25-b9ea-7fa958b91eb1_1432x1491.jpeg)

**Understandably, we used a lot of Brian Eno’s music** **in “Eno”**. Far too much to fit on one soundtrack release. So Universal Music has released four digital EP’s as “bonus scenes” with additional music, and all four are available to stream now on [Apple Music](https://music.apple.com/us/artist/brian-eno/38124), [Spotify](https://open.spotify.com/artist/7MSUfLeTdDEoZiJPDSBXgi), [Tidal](https://tidal.com/browse/artist/9230), and others. These 19 tracks span the breadth of Brian’s music career from his first solo release to his latest album, and make a wonderful companion piece to the official soundtrack. Stream them now wherever you listen to music, or buy the [soundtrack vinyl](https://www.enoshop.co.uk/product/eno_ost?filter=vinyl) from Brian’s site or a record shop near you.
