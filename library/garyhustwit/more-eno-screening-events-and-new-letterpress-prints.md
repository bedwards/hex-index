**We’ve just released two letterpress mini-posters for “Eno”!** These are beautiful prints on 5" x 7" Cranes Lettra 300gsm paper. One features the film’s title treatment by Build (the level of detail in the printing of the tiny credits is unbelievable). The other print features our new Brain One generative machine used to create the film at live screenings, designed by Anamorph and Teenage Engineering.

# More Screening Events This Week

[

![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!mOdc!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Faf7ce52e-c359-4017-a6fc-3326c6a13c3d_5931x3954.jpeg)



](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!mOdc!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Faf7ce52e-c359-4017-a6fc-3326c6a13c3d_5931x3954.jpeg)

**Wow, the last two weeks have been mind-blowing!** Since my last update, we’ve screened “Eno” to sold-out audiences in Toronto, New York City, Los Angeles, Seattle, San Francisco and Portland. It’s been a blast, thank you to the thousands of people who’ve attended so far. And thanks as well to our team, the theater staffs, volunteers and everyone who’s helping make these events possible. Next up: Washington DC! And new events have been announced for Melbourne and the Sheffield Doc Fest. Many more to come.

**May 16 - Washington DC - Miracle Theatre**  
Live screening event and post-film conversation with Gary Hustwit. Co-presented with DC/DOX.  
Get tickets for the [6:30pm](https://www.eventbee.com/v/eno-washington-dc-premiere-630pm/event?eid=246018214) show.

**May 21 - Detroit - Detroit Film Theater**  
Live screening event and post-film conversation with Gary Hustwit  
Get tickets for the [7pm](https://www.eventbee.com/v/enodetroit#/tickets) show.

**May 22 - Milwaukee - Oriental Theatre**  
Live screening event and post-film conversation with Gary Hustwit  
Get tickets for the [7:30pm](https://mkefilm.org/oriental-theatre/events/eno) show.

**May 23 - Chicago - The Music Box**  
Live screening event and post-film conversation with Gary Hustwit and special guests.  
Get tickets for the [7pm](https://www.eventbee.com/v/eno-chicago-premiere-7pm/event?eid=215214074#/tickets) show.

**May 31 to June 2 - Sydney Opera House**  
The Australian Premiere of “Eno” as part of Vivid LIVE, with four live screening events presented by Gary Hustwit.  
[Get tickets.](https://www.sydneyoperahouse.com/vivid-live/film/eno)

**June 3 & 4 - Melbourne - ACMI**  
Two live screenings at the Australian Center for the Moving Image, presented by Gary Hustwit.  
[Get tickets.](https://www.acmi.net.au/whats-on/eno/)

**June 16 - Sheffield Doc Fest**  
A live screening with Director of Programming Brendan Dawes and conversation afterwards.  
[Get tickets](https://www.sheffdocfest.com/composition/eno-live-mix-conversation)

**June 18 to 23 - Nantucket Film Festival**  
A special screening at the Nantucket Film Festival in Massachusetts, post-film Q&A with director Gary Hustwit and producer Jessica Edwards.  
[Get info.](https://nantucketfilmfestival.org/events/eno/)

Stay tuned for news about more UK and European events, and screenings in Vancouver, Minneapolis, Boston, Philly, Atlanta, Stockholm, Amsterdam, Auckland, Barcelona, Tokyo, Italy, and dozens more cities worldwide. We’ll have news on cinema runs and the global streaming release too.
