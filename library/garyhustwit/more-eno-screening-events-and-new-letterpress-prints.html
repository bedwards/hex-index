New “Eno” Letterpress Prints

We’ve just released two letterpress mini-posters for “Eno”! These are beautiful prints on 5" x 7" Cranes Lettra 300gsm paper. One features the film’s title treatment by Build (the level of detail in the printing of the tiny credits is unbelievable). The other print features our new Brain One generative machine used to create the film at live screenings, designed by Anamorph and Teenage Engineering.

Quantities are limited on these, get them now. We’ve also released an official tour poster for “Eno’ which is only available at screenings, so you’ll have to get to one of our events to see those…
