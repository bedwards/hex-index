**Greetings!** I’m psyched to announce the **Japanese theatrical premiere of “Eno”** with special cinema screenings in Tokyo, Nagoya and Osaka in June and July. In order to create multiple unique subtitled versions of the film, we’ve designed a special Japanese build of our generative software system, Brain One. This was a massive undertaking but I can’t wait to share new versions of “Eno” with our friends in Japan. There’s even a [Japanese trailer](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ygxdXRUev68).

The premiere event is **June 21st** in Tokyo, and I’ll be there to present the film. Musicologist **Peter Barakan** will join me in conversation afterwards. Two weeks of screenings will follow in July. Presented with our friends at Beatink and Tokyu Cinemas.

[More info and tickets here](https://enofilm.jp). Arigatō!
