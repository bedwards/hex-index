Now Streaming: Four Bonus EP’s of Music from the Film

Understandably, we used a lot of Brian Eno’s music in “Eno”. Far too much to fit on one soundtrack release. So Universal Music has released four digital EP’s as “bonus scenes” with additional music, and all four are available to stream now on Apple Music, Spotify, Tidal, and others. These 19 tracks span the breadth of Brian’s music career from his first solo release to his latest album, and make a wonderful companion piece to the official soundtrack. Stream them now wherever you listen to music, or buy the soundtrack vinyl from Brian’s site or a record shop near you.
