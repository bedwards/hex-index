**Greetings friends, hope your summer is going well.** I’m dropping into your inbox to let you know about new “Eno” events happening in August and September in various parts of the world. I’m excited for the film to return to **New York City** for an evening of screenings on September 14. And I’m also excited to tease some **major streaming news** that we’ll be announcing later in August! Stay tuned…

Here are some upcoming screenings:

[Get tickets.](https://www.omvf.net/)

# The Final “Eno” Art House Day!

To help make screenings for smaller art houses more feasible, over the past year we’ve offered “Eno Art House Days” where dozens of cinemas in North America, Australia, the UK and elsewhere collectively show a new version of the film. The final installment of this program is on Wednesday, September 17. Art House Day is for paid public events only, and not applicable for film festivals, free events or institutional screenings. The list of cities and theaters will be announced soon. **Want to get a screening in your city? Tell your local indie theater [to go here](https://www.ohyouprettythings.com/shop-old/eno-art-house-day) for info.**

# Only One More Private Screening…

I've been all over the world to show “Eno” over the past year, but we’re still getting a lot of requests to do private screenings for various companies, groups and conferences. I love sharing unique versions of the film and talking about the technology involved and its larger ramifications. But there are only so many places I can physically go!  
  
So I've decided I'll be doing only three private screenings of the film this year, and two of those are already spoken for. **Book the last slot now** at [ohyouprettythings.com](https://www.ohyouprettythings.com/new-products/eno-private-screening-with-gary-hustwit). I'll come to your company, university or conference (or your living room) and create a unique version of the film, and afterwards we can have a conversation about creativity and technology. And I'll bring some posters and other swag for your people too. You can book the last available private “Eno” screening or [get more information here](https://www.ohyouprettythings.com/new-products/eno-private-screening-with-gary-hustwit).
