*“The ultimate secret may well have been cracked by Michael A. Hoffman II in his book, ‘Secret Societies and Psychological Warfare.”*

\-Doug Moench, *Big Book of Conspiracies*

I hope you’re not butthurt by the title, I’m right in this with you. Even for an initiate of conspiratorial literature and revisionist history, *Secret Societies and Psychological Warfare* is hard to stomach and process. The events of history detailed in the book are nothing new: The JFK assassination, NASA conspiracies, Oklahoma City Bombing etc. These are all very familiar topics to this brand of writing but you’ve never read anything about them that took this angle, unless you’re a reader of James Shelby Downer or Hoffman himself you will be enlightened on each page you absorb. Like Dave McGowan’s *Programmed to Kill* that was covered previously in another article of mine, you will find what’s being presented here hard to believe but also hard to argue with.[1](#footnote-1)

Possibly the most important aspect of *Secret* is that much of the conspiratorial culture; in all of its aspects is actually designed to cause despair and helplessness or in modern parlance “blackpilling”. It is also truly remarkable that Hoffman wrote the original addition of the book in 1995 with it being updated in 2001, the book could have been written in 2025 and it would be as relevant, prescient and fresh as it was 30 years ago. Since the books publication, Hoffman has only been proven more correct. Harvard Law professor Cass Sunstein was given a role in the Obama administration as Administrator of The Office of Information and Regulatory Affairs based on his article *“Conspiracy Theories: Cause and Cures”*. Which not only advocates for censorship and “fact-checkers” but the intentional creation of false conspiracies to induce the very mental state Hoffman describes. [2](#footnote-2)[3](#footnote-3)

*“Modern people are fascinated with exposes of sex, violence and Satanism and fascination is itself a form of mind control if we recognize that often times obsessive treatments of these themes under the guise of “exposing them” are the very means of inducing interest and involvement in the occult. People who never hear about Satanism, extreme violence and extreme pornography are unlikely to become involved with Satanism, violence and pornography.”* (pg.34)

Topics like the JFK Assassination or 9/11 can be studied daily for decades or even a lifetime and the “final answer” or resolution will never be found, because it was designed not to be found. The very systems of compartmentalization and plausible deniability ensure this, some people involved may know something or even a great deal about the event but no one person involved knows everything. It is often quite humorous to still here people claim “conspiracies don’t exist” when the very design of governments, militaries, schools, organized crime and corporations are on their face conspiratorial. Is the front desk secretary, janitor and office clerk privy to the same information as the C suite or CEO? According to these dullards how do any of these organizations function without occulting knowledge and information from others within the organization? This meets the very definition of what a conspiracy is possibly minus the illegality of the activity. These large scale events and cataclysms are so wide ranging with so many factors that studying the event becomes a parlor trick to keep the curious researcher guessing and chasing factoids that lead to no resolution. I can admit that I’ve gone down this path at times, I think most in the “awake” crowd have. Hoffman details this periodically throughout the book.

*“It is here necessary to point out a fallacy which has succeeded in defeating many genuine ecological rebels against modern tampering and the occult philosophy and organization which powers it. These are a rare breed to begin with, since the vast majority of researchers who start out in search of the truth about big themes end, like so many other avant-garde thinkers and activists, succumbing to the egregious tyranny of mind-napping that lurks beneath the façade of the liberal occult operation. But a few escape this net only to blunder blindly into another. They become trapped in the fallacy that exposure of the methods and personnel involved in the cryptocracy’s crimes is, by itself, a tremendous step toward overthrowing the power of the cryptocracy “if only we could get some of the facts out in the open” is their refrain. I would reply that the process of Hermetic cryptocracy is not stagnant. It engaged in a remarkable project set into motion millennia ago, an operation which has accomplished most-if not all-of its chief goals with masterful dispatch.”* (pg.51-52)

How many times have we heard a version of the above from the conspiracy crowd? Countless, I’ve said it myself. *“If only enough people know what’s going on that will turn the tide and change things.”* This isn’t a solution, in fact it may actually serve the alchemical masters ruling over us or the cryptocrats as Hoffman terms them. Cryptocracy-*"describes a situation where the actual leadership or ruling class is not publicly accountable or transparent, existing beyond the scrutiny of democratic processes.”* Why does this serve them?

1\. Modern man or the average man today, totally deracinated, Godless in any real sense and beholden to their base passions may actually cause matters to become worse with the full revelation and exposure of this knowledge or “Revelation of The Method”. They may actually admire or envy what their abusers have done to them and want a piece of the action themselves or be so apathetic they don’t even care they’re being abused, a love of their abuser scenario.

2\. John Q public is exposed to blatant criminality on a daily basis from the very officials who are supposed to be our betters while in incredibly few circumstances are any of these people brought to justice. Epstein, Covid, Fast and Furious, Iran-Contra, 9/11, Benghazi, RussiaGate, The Burisma Scandal/Hunter Biden Laptop(s), The Iraq and Afghanistan Wars etc. The list goes on and on and on, when did a government official get charged with a crime let alone fired for any of this? This serves as a psychological warfare operation on the public. A constant reminder that the people ruling over you have all of the power and none of the responsibility that comes with it and laugh at you as they do it. A humiliation ritual.

*“Exposure without action against the perpetrators of the crimes revealed, devolves into a kind of perverse advertisement for the prowess of the cryptocrats, who are seen as having performed fantastic feats of criminal enterprise with a genius that renders them immune from the consequences.”*(p.53)

My goal for the rest of this article and the coming one is to distil the key points from each chapter of *Secret.* This is a difficult task as I have underlines and stars on almost every page of the book but I will do my best, with all that is packed into the book there is no way I will be able to do it full justice. At only 213 pages, including a number of full pages of pictures it is not a time consuming read, get a copy for yourself directly from Hoffman’s website that looks like it was created in 1993 and was never updated.[4](#footnote-4) Interesting side note, there are a number of Hoffman’s books that are banned on Amazon, including *Judaism's Strange Gods.* I don’t think I need to explain why that is banned lol.[5](#footnote-5) Don’t worry you won’t get a virus I promise, ordering it directly from the author is a small step of real action in extricating yourself from “The Videodrome” as Hoffman details.

### Preface to the 1995 Edition

*“There is a joke operating behind the scene-flats of history. It has as its running gag the ambition to perfect mankind and create a utopian society. In 4,000 years of such hucksterage it has not dawned on the particular activists that every attempt to perfect humanity brings only calamity.”* (pg.4)[6](#footnote-6)

*“What separates the traditional people of the past from the modern variety, is their attitude toward human nature. Modern ideologues decree that the trouble with society derives from the traditional view of man as a fallen creature; hopelessly defective. The modernists resolve to improve the world by exalting man and announcing his essential goodness. The Old Christian Way taught that until man faced the fact of his evil nature, he would forever be a prisoner of illusion and of those magicians who would enslave his energies—ostensibly in pursuit of utopia—but actually to the furtherance of the inner executive power ideology of the secret elite.”* (pg.4-5)

Here in the initial pages of the book, Hoffman delivers the root problem. Modern man, self deemed more “enlightened” man has abandoned the past, viewing it as a shackle on his individual development and “progress” that ultimately sees himself as his own god. This is a total inversion of the way The Old Christian, Medieval, or Ancient Man thought, believed and acted in the world. Modern man has continuously bought the lie of The Garden of Eden that came from Satan. *“You can be your own god, why be oppressed and enslaved?”* Modern man is just attempting to repeat the Tower of Babel, its just another version of it that rhymes and just as in that ancient reality of it being destroyed the same will happen to us if nothing changes. [7](#footnote-7)

The solution is not one that modern man will accept because its too painful, too hard and too inconvenient. Adam and Eve were real people, The Garden was a historical reality. They fell due to egoism, pride and disobedience or a lack of humility (The root of all sin). When they fell not only did they in all of their faculties become corrupted and susceptible to sin; the whole of the universe fell from its original harmonious state. All the death, decay and destruction we see today and in the past is a result of The Fall. Man was created to be the priest, prophet and king of the universe God created. This is the very reason why The Angel of Light (Satan) rebelled against God, he would not accept the place Man held and thus he relentlessly torments him. Man retained the Image (reason, free will) and Likeness (potential) from God but our relationship with our Loving Creator was broken with The Fall. Our goal is to repair this relationship through our free will in being obedient to our Loving Creator. This is a process, not a one time decision. As St. Paul says *“For now we see through a glass, darkly; but then face to face: now I know in part; but then shall I know even as also I am known.” (*1 Corinthians 13:12*)*

Due to Adam’s cataclysmic Fall, it took God Himself to become Incarnate to save him, for no fallen man or fallen creation can purify, sanctify, truly illumine and ultimately deify man. Christ, The Logos (Truth Himself), The Second Person of The Godhead had to trample down death by death. Christ is the New or Second Adam. Christ gives us the opportunity to obtain our birthright: Paradise with Him in His Kingdom that is not of this world. This is obtained in this life via ***Theosis*** or synergizing our will to The Will of God via the Grace of the Holy Spirit. [8](#footnote-8)[9](#footnote-9)[10](#footnote-10)

*“God became man so that man might become God.”*

\-St. Athanasius The Great

The very act of God removing Adam and Eve from the Garden was a profound act of mercy, if they would have been allowed to continue to eat of the Tree of Life they would have lived forever in their corrupted state. With this understanding, bodily death (falling asleep until the Second Coming) is a gift from God not a curse. Ponder how far away modern man is from understanding this reality. [11](#footnote-11)
