**What Happened**

Following WW2 and the victory of the Allied Powers, the world was finally “Made Safe for Democracy”. Christian Monarchy’s and Tiny Mustache Man who were “oppressive”, “intolerant of others”, and “backward” were vanquished. The Nuremberg Regime had to be implemented worldwide to ensure nothing like them arose again. And now that the US was a superpower; they had license and cachet to do so. Liberal Globalism at all costs.[1](#footnote-1) [2](#footnote-2) [3](#footnote-3) *“We shall pay any price, bear any burden…”* [4](#footnote-4)

But this didn’t happen easily, Stalin stiff-arming the Marshall Plan forced The Cold War (The US never stopped trade with the USSR-take a look at the various loopholes of the Johnson Act).[5](#footnote-5) [6](#footnote-6) [7](#footnote-7) [8](#footnote-8) This necessitated a constellation of collective security organizations, think tanks, tax-exempt foundations, and policy groups to fight the Soviets, dialectically the *rasion d etre* of these organization was to promote America as “The real freedom fighters and liberators” in opposition to Soviet Authoritarianism.[9](#footnote-9) [10](#footnote-10) The hearts and minds of the rest of the world were up for grabs between the two superpowers. The flywheel was in place for liberalism and democracy to take hold, but this yielded the devastation of *GloboHomo* and *McWorld* (You are going to accept usurious and perpetual debt schemes and degeneracy of all forms or else!). [11](#footnote-11) [12](#footnote-12)Liberalism, as an outgrowth of the Enlightenment has no off switch, it can’t pump the breaks, it must “progress”. It tears out the foundations and roots, it undermines and infects everything it touches. The only way to stop it is by power, force, and a genuine spiritual backbone, not conserving the systems it built. [13](#footnote-13)

1989, The Wall falls, America wins, but this massive apparatus designed to fight the Soviets, along with the Regime in Washington decided they were going to continue fighting the Cold War with no real enemy to fight. Instead of stripping down this apparatus and adjusting to the new world landscape, it remained and grew, swallowing everything in its path. If it was resisted, this same apparatus went to work with the same tools: bribes, sabotage, propaganda, cultural subversion, assassinations, the works. At home, the same methods were used, anyone who opposed “Lib Inc.” was treated as a pariah or worse. Abroad, make-work operations against tactics (terrorism) instead of fighting a clear enemy, not to mention supporting the very terror networks the US military and intelligence astroturfed to eliminate secular/liberal governments became the norm.[14](#footnote-14) [15](#footnote-15)Abroad, as well as at home, the Regime must find ways to justify its existence, problems aren’t allowed to be solved or even discussed, they’re complained about and lectured on. Shrieking against “the other” (anyone who doesn’t support them and obey) as a motus operandi worked for a few decades but this is dying, rapidly. Liberal mouthpieces are saying the same things repeatedly pretending they have the same effect they did ten years ago, deep down they know it’s not working. They have no other means at their disposal. They are akin to the “stuck culture” we see in Hollywood and in the music industry.[16](#footnote-16) [17](#footnote-17)

**The Bright Side**

We have Boomer wine mom’s asking their son’s what the Jewish Question is, it’s wild to contemplate this is where the zeitgeist is currently. A sitting US President is basing policy decisions on how anons on Twitter will react to it. No one thought the cultural shift was going to happen this fast. The picture above is a long series of mask off moments, it’s a good sign in many ways, the DEI hires don’t know how dumb they are and are being left to hang as PR pincushions by a Regime that will be looking for golden parachutes because there are too many holes to plug. Regime blackeyes and optics blunders like this didn’t occur a decade ago. The cat is out of the bag and it’s not going back in.
