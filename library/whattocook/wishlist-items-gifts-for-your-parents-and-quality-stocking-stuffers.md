Parents can be hard to shop for — my mom has told me countless times, “I don’t need a thing!” And while that may be true, I still want to gift them something special! So I aim to gift my mom (and dad, though I put a lot of those ideas on our **[guide for the guys](https://whattocook.substack.com/p/cozy-gifts-gifts-for-guys-and-gifts)**!) gifts that are both special and useful, like my favorite EVOO; a photo frame with always-up-to-date pictures of their grandsons; cute, comfy shoes; and a calendar that doubles as a piece of art. Here are all of my tried-and-true parent gifts!

When their friends come over they love this thing, too!
    
2.  Don’t underestimate the power of **[colored tape](https://urlgeni.us/amzn/4wzPd4)**.
    
3.  My boys absolutely love making little creatures of **[Air Dough](https://urlgeni.us/amzn/hWr_r7)**. Would be cute to stuff their stockings full of these!
