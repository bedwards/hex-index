Hi Friends,

This week, it’s quieter in the world of remote editorial work as many companies prepare to hit snooze for the rest of the year. Still, there are some great opportunities, including paid positions with the *Paris Review*, Hatchette Book Group, as well as post-MFA fellowships, internships, and part-time gigs. You’ll find lots of lit mags looking to add to their volunteer reader team this time of year.

As the year draws to a close, my past life as a farmer has me thinking about the cycles of life and how the proverbial field needs to lay fallow to let the soil regenerate. As with all living things, we need periods of rest and rejuvenation, even some silence every now and again. This might be a great time to polish your resumé and sharpen your pencils so that you can be ready for new work opportunities in January.

Here at Chill Subs, we are preparing for hibernation mode ourselves for the last two weeks of the year. **Since we won’t be around at the end of the month, you’ll find fellowships and residencies, internships, and volunteer lit mag gigs below!**

Next week, I’ll send one more paying pitch call newsletter to your inbox before we take a break. In the meantime, maybe one of these opportunities is made just for you.

**This week we have:**

-   9 Remote & Hybrid Jobs
    
-   4 Freelance, Contract, and/or Part-time
    
-   4 Fellowships & Residencies
    
-   2 Teaching Jobs
    
-   2 Internships
    
-   13 Lit Mag & Volunteer Gigs
    
-   4 BONUS Paying Pitch Calls
