Well, this year we did it again, but differently, and betterly (better-er?). In case you missed the announcements on our Chill Subs newsletter or socials, here’s a quick breakdown along with the top 10 in each category.

And, if you’re into deeper stats and want to learn more about how we designed this year’s Best Lit Mags Community Awards, pop over to our blog here.

In brief, though—for our winners, we took the tag each magazine was awarded the most, and ranked them within that category. This way, the resulting winners would be those who most represent that core aspect of the community we want to support and see more of. Since 8,160 people voted for 2,299 magazines 13,884 times(!!), we ended up with roughly 36,173 tags awarded to lit mags. So we feel pretty confident that the folks who landed at the top of these categories deserve to be there.
