Hi Friends,

I’m grateful for you and for the internet. Yes, wonderful you. And yes, that awful wasteland of dystopian dopamine manipulation known as the internet. I’m grateful that despite the gradual decay of the modern world, we live in an era where writers can connect with potential outlets for their work without having to send messages in bottles, train homing pigeons to find the office windows of editors, or (gasp) go to the post office to mail manila envelopes with SASE’s included.

This week, we’re sending 26 paying pitch calls to your inbox, where you might even hear back before the new year about your next paid assignment. Because if there’s one thing the internet is still good at is increasing the speed of communication.

Some of these pitch deadlines are coming up quickly. So, seize them before they expire. Go make the internet a better place with your writing, friends.
