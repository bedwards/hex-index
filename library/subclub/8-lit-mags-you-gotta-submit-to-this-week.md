**[HOOT](https://chillsubs.com/magazine/hoot) → Deadline: Nov 27** **| Fee:** Yes **| Pay:** No **| Open** for Poetry **|** ☑︎ Sim Subs **|** ☑︎ Some Reprints **|** **R:** 180 days **|** **A:** 16.33% **|** 3K+ followers **|** United States **|** 2011 — *“HOOT is a postcard. A very nice-looking one. With writing on it! It is also a little more complicated than that. It is a brief, displayable, shareable literary magazine.”*

[

![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!4mfe!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F220ddd6d-22e5-44ef-9f1c-ae7b26490b42_1973x911.png)

**Our one cent:** I’m a sucker for good print lit mags and while HOOT also has a stellar digital presence, their print idea is so cool! They send out monthly print issues on a literal postcard. How freakin’ awesome is that? Honestly, this is the kind of sweet and out of the box innovations I’d love to see more of in lit mag land.
