Every year, our community votes for their favorite lit mags in [the Best Lit Mag Awards](https://www.chillsubs.com/best-lit-mag-awards). Guess what time it is?

This award has no fees. No judges. Nothing matters but what the community votes (which is still legal, we think). It is the only literary award for magazines that is given by the community, rather than judges or grants.

Winning lit mags receive $300 each and get showered with love & attention across social media & Substack.

Vote and you’ll be entered to win $500, plus we’re randomly drawing 10 Sub Club Subscribers[1](#footnote-1) to comp a 1-year subscription to.

**Voting takes under a minute and closes December 7th. Let’s show some love to the mags that deserve it.**
