Greetings, friends, from a windblown landscape dotted with red and yellow leaves. Outside my window is a world ready for change. Meanwhile, I’m getting over my third flu/cold/plague in 6 weeks (because I drop my kiddo off at a petri dish three times a week). There’s nothing like lounging in a blanket with sleeves with a trusty red dog warming your toes, breathing in the steam of a hot cup of tea, and sending out resumes. Ahhh, fall.

While, some of us might be winding down our job searches, others may just be getting started, hoping to land that perfect gig to begin the new year one step closer to being a new you…us…? In any case, I wanted to focus on jobs that don’t require moving to NYC or LA for those of us who can only handle so much change at a time.

Unless, you already lived there—in which case, I’ve included some hybrid jobs for you.

This week, I bring you a focus on ***remote & hybrid jobs*** that you can do from anywhere, even while wearing your favorite blanket with sleeves. Because in this economy, it’s nice to be able to do something on our own terms.
