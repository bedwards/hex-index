Here’s a list of longevity science and industry resources to get you started. If you have any suggestions please leave them in the comments below.

## Blogs / Posts

-   [Laura Deming’s Short Longevity FAQ](https://www.ldeming.com/longevityfaq) - A > 10 min read on the basics of longevity science.
    
-   [Jose Luis Ricon’s Longer Longevity FAQ](https://nintil.com/longevity/) - A more in-depth read on the biology of aging.
    
-   [Hallmarks of Aging](https://www.cell.com/cell/fulltext/S0092-8674\(13\)00645-4) - A review (2013) of nine different proposed mechanisms of aging. Cited by everyone but getting a little old. There’s still a lot of debate over what causes aging, but these hallmark targets are a good start and some are well-validated in mice (and being tested in humans today).
    
-   [Fight Aging! Blog and Newsletter](https://www.fightaging.org/) - by Reason. The newsletter is a weekly summary of the newest anti-aging science papers and news.
    
-   [How to build a biotech](https://www.celinehh.com/how-to-build-a-biotech) - Celine Halioua’s writings. (Founder @ Loyal)
    
-   [How to start a biotech company in the longevity industry](https://www.fightaging.org/pdf/how-to-start-a-biotech-company-in-the-longevity-industry.pdf) - a guide written by Reason (Fight Aging! / Repair Biotechnologies)

## Books

-   [Juvenescence: Investing in the Age of Longevity](https://www.amazon.com/Juvenescence-Investing-longevity-Jim-Mellon-ebook/dp/B075WXX93Q/ref=sr_1_1?dchild=1&keywords=juvenescence&qid=1611034623&sr=8-1) - written by Jim Mellon, patron saint of longevity investing / British billionaire. Covers both science and industry.
    
-   [Lifespan: Why we age — and why we don’t have to](https://www.amazon.com/Lifespan-audiobook/dp/B07QGH1Q43/ref=sr_1_1?dchild=1&keywords=Lifespan&qid=1611034648&sr=8-1) - written by David Sinclair, professor of Genetics at Harvard. A good book for general audiences.
    
-   [Ending Aging](https://www.amazon.com/Ending-Aging-audiobook/dp/B01EBAP43C/ref=sr_1_1?dchild=1&keywords=Aubrey+de+grey&qid=1611036286&refresh=1&sr=8-1) - written by Aubrey de Grey, director of SENS Research Foundation. The book that started the revolution for many people (including myself)
    
-   [Replacing Aging](http://replacing aging) - written by Jean Hebert, professor of neuroscience and genetics at Albert Einstein College of Medicine.
