##### A man without a plan and a man with a plan

# Underwhelming Trump-Xi meeting

*October 30*—Donald Trump met with Xi Jinping in Busan, South Korea for a highly anticipated meeting that was supposed to result in a trade agreement. Trump told reporters flying with him back to Washington: “On a scale from zero to 10, with 10 being the best, I would say the meeting was a 12.”

Xi Jinping did not seem to enjoy the encounter as much, judging from his [pained facial expression and silence as Trump babbled on](https://www.youtube.com/shorts/53Qah7lBlig) during a pre-meeting press event. And all the meeting actually achieved was to pause recently threatened U.S. tariffs and delay the implementation of China’s [new export controls on rare earths](https://www.thechinaweek.com/p/chinas-leverage-access-to-its-factories). It’s just back to the status quo of a few months ago.
