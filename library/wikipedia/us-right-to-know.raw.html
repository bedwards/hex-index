U.S. Right To Know (USRTK) is a nonprofit organization. It was founded in 2014 by Gary Ruskin, and the organization is primarily funded by Organic Consumers Association. The organization is known for anti-GMO activism, and for promoting the COVID-19 lab leak theory.

In 2025, USRTK filed a public records lawsuit against the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill for withholding many research documents belonging to Ralph Baric.
