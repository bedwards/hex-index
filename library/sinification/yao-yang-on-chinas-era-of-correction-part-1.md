*Today’s edition opens with an introduction by [Gerard DiPippo](https://www.rand.org/about/people/d/dipippo_gerard.html), Senior Researcher at RAND and Associate Director of the RAND China Research Center. He was previously a Senior Geo-Economics Analyst at Bloomberg Economics, a Senior Fellow at CSIS, and served for 11 years in the U.S. Intelligence Community. His research focuses on China’s economy, industrial policy, financial system, and its ties with the United States. Gerard’s latest article on [China’s economy amid the ongoing Sino–U.S. trade war](https://www.prcleader.org/post/changing-course-in-a-storm-china-s-economy-in-the-trade-war) is a must-read. I am delighted to have him share a few thoughts on this excellent and wonderfully unvarnished lecture by the one and only Yao Yang. — Thomas*
