**The Contrarian** is a weekly newsletter by **[Kashish Mehta](https://twitter.com/itsKashishMehta).**

This newsletter revolves around providing a fresh and quite often contrary take on topics such as Entrepreneurship, Personal Finance, Self Help and Politics.

The purpose of this newsletter is to provide a perspective that may not always be the most popular or widely accepted one, but one that might pique your curiosity.

If you are someone that believes that the grass is not always greener on the other side, then this is the newsletter for you. And if you think, it is greener then allow me to show you the other side.

Sometimes you might also come across a little bit of sarcasm and humour. Got both these qualities from my mom: more sarcasm and less humour 😜 .

The witty humour and taunting sarcasm will add a personal touch to your reading experience, I hope…

Anyway, enjoy reading and feel free to share your feedback and comments.

Cheers,

Kashish
