When I begin writing an article, it usually takes me two or three attempts to finally hit a current that takes the reader all the way through to the end. But this time it was different. Guess how many times I started all over again with this article about the meaning of the Saturn-Neptune conjunction in Aries, the signature constellation of 2025 and 2026? At least 10 times! Until I realized that this is exactly the big question for this and next year: How should we begin? Whether as an individual, an organization or a society – how should we begin something new?
