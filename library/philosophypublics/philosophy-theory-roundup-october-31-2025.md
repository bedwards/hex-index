##### ROUNDUP

1.  [Part 5: On the Limit in Kant](https://kosmotheoros.substack.com/p/part-5-on-the-limit-in-kant) (3 likes): The sublime. By in *Kosmotheoros*.
    
2.  [The role of philosophy (and other humanities) in perilous times](https://ericalucaststonestreet.substack.com/p/the-role-of-philosophy-in-perilous) (11 likes): A map and a compass. By in *Humaning is Hard, but Philosophy Can Help*.
    
3.  [The Compute-Industrial Complex](https://www.technostatecraft.com/p/the-compute-industrial-complex) (7 likes): Inside some of the coalitions turning America’s AI boom into a new model of infrastructural enclosure, deregulation, and state-capital coordination. By in *Techno-Statecraft*.
    
4.  [If humans went extinct, could we re-evolve?](https://miniphilosophy.substack.com/p/if-humans-went-extinct-could-we-re) (11 likes): Maybe — just don’t expect a carbon copy. By and in *Mini Philosophy*.
    
5.  [What is Eastern mysticism? Beyond the Opposites](https://romaricjannel.substack.com/p/what-is-eastern-mysticism-beyond) (10 likes): Fritjof Capra, “The Tao of Physics: An Exploration of the Parallels Between Modern Physics and Eastern Mysticism” (1975) #8. By in *Philosophy and Beyond*.
    
6.  [Me trying to write like Frank O’Hara](https://theexistentialreader.substack.com/p/me-trying-to-write-like-frank-ohara) (3 likes). By in *The Existential Reader*.
    
7.  [An Existential Guide to: Love](https://theshadowedarchive.substack.com/p/an-existential-guide-to-love) (356 likes): In 3 parts (like my heart after my first girlfriend left me) xD. By in *The Shadowed Archive*.
    
8.  [Accelerationism and Political Violence](https://danieltutt.substack.com/p/accelerationism-and-political-violence) (13 likes): Join me for a live event in DC + an excerpt from my book on Right and Left Accelerationism. By in *Daniel’s Journal*.
    
9.  [Intimate Relationships course: check-in and Q&A](https://substack.com/home/post/p-176869066) (15 likes): A recording from Ellie Anderson’s live video. By in *Intimate Reltionships*.
    
10.  [User Not Found](https://schoolofattention.substack.com/p/user-not-found) (11 likes): On the ground at Delete Day with Nick Plante. By attention in *The Empty Cup*.
     
11.  [Uncovering histories of the targeting and policing of Muslims in Kenya](https://www.radicalbookscollective.com/p/uncovering-histories-of-targeting) (18 likes): A conversation with scholar Samar Al-Bulushi about her powerful book “War-Making as Worldmaking: Kenya, United States and the War on Terror.”. By in *Radical Books Collective*.
     
12.  [The God of Too Much](https://zahrahaider.substack.com/p/the-god-of-too-much) (3 likes): A love letter to the women who never got to be angry out loud. By in *ZERO POINT*.
     
13.  [Migration and Self-Mutilation as Protest](https://everydayinquiry.substack.com/p/migration-and-self-mutilation-as) (3 likes): By K. T. Mills. By in *Everyday Analysis Substack*.
     
14.  [The Ideal of Self-Governance](https://cyrilhedoin.substack.com/p/the-ideal-of-self-governance) (5 likes): For a Tocquevillian Political Economy. By @cyrilhedoin in *The Archimedean Point*.
     
15.  [Notes From Ian McEwan’s What We Can Know](https://gmwrites.substack.com/p/notes-from-ian-mcewans-what-can-we) (12 likes): On what we can know, what we should preserve, and why the humanities matter... By in *g.m.*.
     
16.  [Free Will and Violence](https://matthewgindin.substack.com/p/free-will-and-violence) (11 likes): Spinoza Versus Buddhist Scripture: Fight! .... But Nonviolently. By in *Philosophy As Therapy*.
     
17.  [145\. AI Chatbots](https://overthinkpod.substack.com/p/145-ai-chatbots) (5 likes). By in *Overthink Podcast*.
     
18.  [“Imaginative contagion and moral corruption” - Alex Fisher (University of Leeds)](https://newworkinphilosophy.substack.com/p/imaginative-contagion-and-moral-corruption) (8 likes): Philosophy and Phenomenological Research (Early View). By in *New Work In Philosophy*.
     
19.  [Real Dehumanization](https://davidlivingstonesmith.substack.com/p/real-dehumanization) (2 likes): My 2024 Lebowitz Prize Lecture. By in *Dehumanization Matters*.
     
20.  [5 things germ season can teach us about capitalism](https://laurabasu.substack.com/p/5-things-germ-season-can-teach-us) (20 likes): We’ve solved our childcare problem and have now all gone down with some horrid bug. Obvs. By in *Loveconomics*.
     
21.  [What is civil society, and why should we care?](https://www.programmablemutter.com/p/what-is-civil-society-and-why-should) (93 likes): Ernest Gellner on the conditions of liberty. By in *Programmable Mutter*.
     
22.  [AI vs AI](https://www.newcartographies.com/p/ai-vs-ai) (84 likes): Slop in, slop out. By in *New Cartographies*.
     
23.  [Sapiosexuality isn’t a Trend](https://helenhigginsbooks1.substack.com/p/sapiosexuality-is-sacred) (105 likes): Desiring intelligence isn’t just for algorithms and likes. Here’s why. By in *Helen Higgins*.
     
24.  [The Pleasures of Excess](https://philosophypublics.substack.com/p/the-pleasures-of-excess) (5 likes): Thinking broadly about Linda Williams’ piece on “body genres” (e.g., horror, porn, and melodrama), and the politics of representation. By in *Philosophy Publics*.
     
25.  [Death is universal, but our choices are personal.](https://hellomortal.substack.com/p/death-is-universal-but-our-choices) (26 likes): Dr. Shoshana Ungerleider on loss, love, and living in the face of mortality. By in *Hello Mortal*.
