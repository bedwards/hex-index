##### ROUNDUP

1.  [The Most Dangerous Animal](https://davidlivingstonesmith.substack.com/p/the-most-dangerous-animal) (3 likes): A Powerful Short Film by the Late Al Sutton. By in *Dehumanization Matters*.
    
2.  [Spinoza’s Recapitulation of Parmenides](https://matthewgindin.substack.com/p/spinozas-recapitulation-of-parmenides) (4 likes): the eternal positivity of |Being, the gift of reason, and its casualties. By in *Philosophy As Therapy*.
    
3.  [If you learn how to win, you need to learn when to stop.](https://miniphilosophy.substack.com/p/the-genius-and-the-demon) (31 likes): Virtues need virtues to stay virtues. By and in *Mini Philosophy*.
    
4.  [Capitalism and Mental Health](https://theexistentialreader.substack.com/p/capitalism-and-mental-health) (12 likes): The effects of capitalism on mental health as diagnosed by Mark Fisher. By in *The Existential Reader*.
    
5.  [Privacy as Jihad](https://thecyberhermetica.substack.com/p/privacy-as-jihad) (7 likes): A discipline to protect the human principle from algorithmic colonization. By in *The Cyber Hermetica*.
    
6.  [147\. Confidence](https://overthinkpod.substack.com/p/147-confidence) (4 likes). By in *Overthink Podcast*.
    
7.  [Beyond the Gaze of the Other](https://deterritorialization.substack.com/p/beyond-the-gaze-of-the-other) (17 likes): A critique of Levinas’s deification of the Other. By in *Deterritorialization*.
    
8.  [Nov 30, 2025: Reading Palestine 5 - We Are Not Numbers: Voices of Gaza’s Youth](https://www.radicalbookscollective.com/p/nov-30-2025-reading-palestine-5-we) (2 likes). By in *Radical Books Collective*.
    
9.  [Stocks Aren’t Salvation](https://www.hamiltonnolan.com/p/stocks-arent-salvation) (150 likes): Political consequences of the ownership society. By in *How Things Work*.
    
10.  [Jesus and the Doomsday Clock, part 3: The All-American Endtime Show](https://heyslick.substack.com/p/jesus-and-the-doomsday-clock-3) (24 likes): How Apocalyptic Christianity Became America’s New Theology of Power. By in *Hey Slick*.
     
11.  [What happens when the person you’re grieving is you](https://hellomortal.substack.com/p/what-happens-when-the-person-youre-a85) (54 likes): Becoming ourselves means saying goodbye to the selves we’ve outgrown. By in *Hello Mortal*.
     
12.  [The Relativity of Space and Time](https://romaricjannel.substack.com/p/the-relativity-of-space-and-time) (12 likes): Fritjof Capra, “The Tao of Physics: An Exploration of the Parallels Between Modern Physics and Eastern Mysticism” (1975) #9. By in *Philosophy and Beyond*.
     
13.  [Red Thunder, Black Borders: The Optic Power of Navajo Textiles](https://roguearthistorian.substack.com/p/red-thunder-black-borders-the-optic) (21 likes): #nativeamericanheritagemonth. By in *Rogue Art Historian*.
     
14.  [If you’re feeling lost, read this.](https://helenhigginsbooks1.substack.com/p/if-youre-feeling-lost-read-this) (68 likes): Between the breakdown and the breakthrough, we’re all just looking for the keys to life. By in *Helen Higgins*.
     
15.  [The Trillion-Dollar Vassal](https://www.theorybrief.com/p/the-trillion-dollar-vassal) (2 likes): How Trump 2.0, Israel’s Gaza war, and Norway’s two-trillion-dollar sovereign wealth fund converged in a firestorm of finance, geopolitics, and genocide. By in *The Theory Brief*.
     
16.  [The relational conception of the self: historical threads, part 3](https://ericalucaststonestreet.substack.com/p/the-relational-conception-of-the-e0f) (6 likes): Anna Julia Cooper on identity-based social ties. By in *Humaning is Hard, but Philosophy Can Help*.
     
17.  [How capitalism gave me an autoimmune disease](https://laurabasu.substack.com/p/how-capitalism-is-giving-us-autoimmune) (26 likes): Part 1: trauma. By in *Loveconomics*.
     
18.  [A quick note about this newsletter](https://wellreadherring.substack.com/p/a-quick-note-about-this-newsletter) (10 likes). By in *Well-Read Herring*.
     
19.  [Andrew on Andrew](https://anro865554.substack.com/p/andrew-on-andrew-9ef): Pt 2. By in *The Existential Pulse*.
     
20.  [The Structural Violence of Risk Management Behind the AI Infrastructure Bubble](https://www.technostatecraft.com/p/the-structural-violence-of-risk-management) (13 likes): How financial institutions sustain the AI boom by exporting risks to everyone else. By in *Techno-Statecraft*.
     
21.  [Out of Love for All That Is Human](https://schoolofattention.substack.com/p/out-of-love-for-all-that-is-human) (17 likes): On the future of the humanities with Justin Smith-Ruiu (& co.). By and in *The Empty Cup*.
     
22.  [Epistemic Infrastructure: Building Shared Truth in an Era of Disaggregation](https://harmoniousdiscourse.substack.com/p/epistemic-infrastructure-building) (13 likes). By in *The Structural Lens*.
     
23.  [Imaginary Truths](https://ellismarte.substack.com/p/imaginary-truths). By in *Ellis’s Substack*.
     
24.  [Is romantic love oppressive? Berit Brogaard: Lecture 9, Intimate Relationships](https://substack.com/home/post/p-178719217) (7 likes): Or is it rather attachment love that holds us back? By in *Intimate Reltionships*.
     
25.  [Moby-Dick](https://samantharosehill.substack.com/p/moby-dick) (17 likes): Week 1. By in *Reflections by Samantha Rose Hill*.
     
26.  [Tyrique Mack-Georges](https://celineleboeuf.substack.com/p/tyrique-mack-georges) (11 likes): Philosophy adopts a critical attitude toward what is normally taken for granted. By in *Why Philosophy?*.
     
27.  [Does capital really need a vast labor market?](https://marxandfriends.substack.com/p/does-capital-really-need-a-vast-labor) (17 likes): Entertaining a thought experiment. By in *Marx and Friends*.
     
28.  [ways of not seeing](https://www.late-review.com/p/ways-of-not-seeing) (106 likes): i am obsessed with the eye doctor. By in *the late review*.
     
29.  [What European Pragmatic Federalism Would Look Like](https://cyrilhedoin.substack.com/p/what-european-pragmatic-federalism) (10 likes): And Why It’s Difficult to Achieve. By in *The Archimedean Point*.
     
30.  [Maybe we need a Butlerian Jihad after all](https://jaredhenderson.substack.com/p/maybe-we-need-a-butlerian-jihad-after) (104 likes): AI won’t bring your dead loved ones back. By in *Commonplace Philosophy*.
     
31.  [The Desire to Create Art, Not Content](https://julianphilosophy.substack.com/p/the-desire-to-create-art-not-content) (71 likes): Substack is changing. But must we change with it? By in *Julian de Medeiros*.
