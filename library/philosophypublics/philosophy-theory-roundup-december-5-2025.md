##### ROUNDUP

1.  [The Dynamic Universe](https://romaricjannel.substack.com/p/the-dynamic-universe) : Fritjof Capra, “The Tao of Physics: An Exploration of the Parallels Between Modern Physics and Eastern Mysticism” (1975) #10. By in *Philosophy and Beyond*.
    
2.  [Is it morally acceptable to put your family first?](https://miniphilosophy.substack.com/p/is-it-okay-to-prejudice-against-strangers): The philosophy of blameless wrongdoing. By and in *Mini Philosophy*.
    
3.  [Reject AI “Art”](https://www.jphilll.com/p/reject-ai-art): Resist the ceding of your soul. By in *New Means*.
    
4.  [Rebuilding the Productive Middle Class: A Nonpartisan Blueprint for Income, Assets, and Opportunity](https://harmoniousdiscourse.substack.com/p/rebuilding-the-productive-middle). By in *The Structural Lens*.
    
5.  [Large Language Models As The Tales That Are Sung](https://www.programmablemutter.com/p/large-language-models-as-the-tales): Gene Wolfe, Albert Lord, machine culture. By in *Programmable Mutter*.
    
6.  [Dehumanization and the Horror of Killing](https://davidlivingstonesmith.substack.com/p/dehumanization-and-the-horror-of). By in *Dehumanization Matters*.
    
7.  [Where Is My Mind? Bernard Stiegler and the Fight for Our Memory](https://heyslick.substack.com/p/where-is-my-mind-bernard-stiegler): On human nature, technics, and stupidity. By in *Hey Slick*.
    
8.  [Culture Wars Are For Cowards](https://www.hamiltonnolan.com/p/culture-wars-are-for-cowards): And most people don’t like bullies. By in *How Things Work*.
    
9.  [150\. Aztec Philosophy with Sebastian Purcell (Extended)](https://overthinkpod.substack.com/p/150-aztec-philosophy-with-sebastian) . By in *Overthink Podcast*.
    
10.  [The Derveni Papyrus](https://ideasroadshow.substack.com/p/the-derveni-papyrus) : A Conversation with Richard Janko, Professor of Classical Studies, University of Michigan. By in *Ideas Roadshow Behind The Lens*.
     
11.  [Irving Singer, Love as Valuing: Lecture 12, Intimate Relationships](https://substack.com/home/post/p-178719818): What if love isn’t an emotion or a matter of union, but a way of valuing someone? By in *Intimate Relationships*.
     
12.  [Liberation From Education?](https://peacefulrevolutionary.substack.com/p/liberation-from-education): Learning About The Liberationists (Satire) - Part 5. By in *Free Society*.
     
13.  [Grateful for what we don’t yet have](https://hellomortal.substack.com/p/grateful-for-what-we-dont-yet-have): The dark side of manifestation, and the ancient Japanese practice that breaks the cycle of wanting and lack. By and and in *Hello Mortal*.
     
14.  [Evolution is a Fact, not “Just a Theory”](https://billvanderburgh.substack.com/p/evolution-is-a-fact-not-just-a-theory): Or, how to stop making silly claims in religious apologetics arguments. By in *PHILOSOPHY FOR THE PUBLIC GOOD*.
     
15.  [Christmas lights and free riding](https://ericalucaststonestreet.substack.com/p/christmas-lights-and-free-riders): Why do I feel like I have a duty to decorate? By in *Humaning is Hard, but Philosophy Can Help*.
     
16.  [Death and the Social](https://danieltutt.substack.com/p/death-and-the-social) : The Social Question in the Twenty-First Century. By in *Daniel’s Journal*.
     
17.  [Populism for Realists](https://cyrilhedoin.substack.com/p/populism-for-realists): Fast-thinking, Ignorance, and Irrationality are not Enough to Understand Contemporary Populism. By in *The Archimedean Point*.
     
18.  [Twelve Questions For Spinoza, Finit](https://matthewgindin.substack.com/p/twelve-questions-for-spinoza-finit): the final four. By in *Philosophy As Therapy*.
     
19.  [#7 Jon Mills: The Psychology Behind Our Self-Destructive Civilisation](https://www.thelivingphilosophy.com/p/7-jon-mills-the-psychology-behind). By in *The Living Philosophy*.
     
20.  [On Zarah Sultana](https://theexistentialreader.substack.com/p/on-zarah-sultana). By in *The Existential Reader*.
     
21.  [Fossil AI—Finance Capital’s New Utility Frontier](https://www.technostatecraft.com/p/fossil-aifinance-capitals-new-utility): Private equity, utilities, and regulators gamble on plausibly exaggerated data center forecasts and long-lived fossil assets. By in *Techno-Statecraft*.
     
22.  [Music and the End of Aristotle’s Politics](https://jaredhenderson.substack.com/p/music-and-the-end-of-aristotles-politics): Aristotle’s Politics, Book 8. By in *Commonplace Philosophy*.
