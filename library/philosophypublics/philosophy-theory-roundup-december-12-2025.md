##### ROUNDUP

1.  [Holding Love & Power (Without Losing Either)](https://lovephilosophy.substack.com/p/holding-love-and-power-without-losing): Eros as world creator, the love beyond it, and ontological meta-modernism with policy advisor Jamie Bristow. By @andreahiott and in *Love & Philosophy*.
    
2.  [Post-Empire Blues: Collapse, Survival, and a Radical Hope](https://medievalmusings.substack.com/p/post-empire-blues-collapse-survival): Book Club | December 2025 | Section 5. By in *Medieval Musings*.
    
3.  [How having a “silent vocation” can be a way to live a rich life](https://julianphilosophy.substack.com/p/how-having-a-silent-vocation-can): getting settled in LA. By in *Julian de Medeiros*.
    
4.  [Playing Attention](https://schoolofattention.substack.com/p/playing-attention): Toying with the work of make believe. By in *The Empty Cup*.
    
5.  [Nick Fuentes and the Platforming of Extremism:](https://theexistentialreader.substack.com/p/nick-fuentes-and-the-platforming) : Free Speech as Commodity Fetishism. By in *The Existential Reader*.
    
6.  [What is sabbatical for?](https://ericalucaststonestreet.substack.com/p/what-is-sabbatical-for): The meaning of work. By in *Humaning is Hard, but Philosophy Can Help*.
    
7.  [Political Violence and the Other](https://ellismarte.substack.com/p/political-violence-and-the-other-ef6). By in *Ellis’s Substack*.
    
8.  [Mired In The Dark Ages](https://ideasroadshow.substack.com/p/mired-in-the-dark-ages): Why do so many art historians ignore digital media technology? By in *Ideas Roadshow Behind The Lens*.
    
9.  [Folk Songs, Fake Traditions: Romantic Nationalism and the Art of Beautiful Lies](https://roguearthistorian.substack.com/p/folk-songs-fake-traditions-romantic). By in *Rogue Art Historian*.
    
10.  [the mirror-self](https://www.late-review.com/p/the-mirror-self): an obsession. By in *the late review*.
     
11.  [The era of jobs is ending](https://www.thepavement.xyz/p/the-era-of-jobs-is-ending): As it fucking should. By in *Beneath the Pavement*.
     
12.  [5 Recent Books on Overthink](https://overthinkpod.substack.com/p/5-recent-books-on-overthink). By in *Overthink Podcast*.
     
13.  [America has identified its greatest enemy: Western Europe](https://www.programmablemutter.com/p/america-has-identified-its-final): Trump’s new National Security Strategy: what if groypers cosplayed George Kennan? By in *Programmable Mutter*.
     
14.  [Foolosophy: Why reading philosophy on TikTok beats teaching it at university](https://theorygang.substack.com/p/foolosophy-why-reading-philosophy): Week 35: Experimenting with Absurdism. By in *Theory Gang*.
     
15.  [The Concept Album: Inspirations](https://kellystith.substack.com/p/the-concept-album-inspirations). By in *The Way Out Is Through*.
     
16.  [ANNOUNCEMENT: Enigmas and Failed Interpretations](https://rafaelholmberg.substack.com/p/announcement-enigmas-and-failed-interpretations): Booklet Publication and Free Copy for Paid Subscribers. By in *Antagonisms of the Everyday: Philosophy, Culture, Politics*.
     
17.  [New Orleans Is Watching You, Fuckers](https://www.hamiltonnolan.com/p/new-orleans-is-watching-you-fuckers): As the feds chase immigrants, a city chases the feds. By in *How Things Work*.
     
18.  [Nietzsche Within the Left: The Methods of Foucault & Deleuze vs. Huey Newton](https://danieltutt.substack.com/p/nietzsche-within-the-left-the-methods): Enjoy an early release of my seminar. By in *Daniel’s Journal*.
     
19.  [Liberation From Illness?](https://peacefulrevolutionary.substack.com/p/liberation-from-illness): Learning About The Liberationists (Satire) - Part 6. By in *Free Society*.
     
20.  [The age you feel vs. the age you are](https://hellomortal.substack.com/p/the-age-you-feel-vs-the-age-you-are): A reflection on the strange experience of getting older, and what science says about it. By and and in *Hello Mortal*.
     
21.  [“Pluribus” and Philosophy Part Four](https://billvanderburgh.substack.com/p/pluribus-and-philosophy-part-four): A Philosopher Watches Sci Fi: Why not eat the dead? By in *PHILOSOPHY FOR THE PUBLIC GOOD*.
     
22.  [A Sovereign Decision: How Crisis Politics Became Permanent Governance](https://harmoniousdiscourse.substack.com/p/a-sovereign-decision-how-crisis-politics). By in *The Structural Lens*.
     
23.  [B. Scot Rousse](https://celineleboeuf.substack.com/p/b-scot-rousse): Philosophy is an opening to wonder, inspiration, and participation in the adventure of being human. By in *Why Philosophy?*.
     
24.  [La Petite Mort](https://helenhigginsbooks1.substack.com/p/la-petite-mort): A brief essay on pleasure and small deaths. By in *Helen Higgins*.
     
25.  [“We’re all heading to Gaza…That’s the model for the world.” - Ammiel Alcalay](https://www.radicalbookscollective.com/p/were-all-heading-to-gazathats-the): A conversation with Ammiel Alcalay contextualizing the present moment as genocide continues in Palestine, as American universities unravel, and the grip of authoritarianism tightens worldwide. By in *Radical Books Collective*.
     
26.  [Before Trustworthiness: Trustability](https://romaricjannel.substack.com/p/before-trustworthiness-trustability): From a public discussion to a peer‑reviewed paper. By in *Philosophy and Beyond*.
     
27.  [Why capitalism is inherently ableist](https://laurabasu.substack.com/p/why-capitalism-is-inherently-ableist): Part 1: the roots and the structure. By in *Loveconomics*.
     
28.  [The Best Philosophy Lectures on YouTube](https://jaredhenderson.substack.com/p/the-best-philosophy-lectures-on-youtube): A living resource for your self-education. By in *Commonplace Philosophy*.
     
29.  [Book of the Dreamer](https://theshadowedarchive.substack.com/p/book-of-the-dreamer). By in *The Shadowed Archive*.
     
30.  [bell hooks, Love and abuse cannot coexist: Lecture 14, Intimate Relationships](https://substack.com/home/post/p-181266073) : Getting into hooks’s All About Love through the family dynamics of Breaking Bad. By in *Intimate Relationships*.
     
31.  [The Ethics Versus the Politics of Imperceptible Effects](https://cyrilhedoin.substack.com/p/the-ethics-versus-the-politics-of): A Matter of Individual Responsibility and Accountability. By in *The Archimedean Point*.
