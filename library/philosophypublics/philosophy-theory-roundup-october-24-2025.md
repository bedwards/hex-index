##### ROUNDUP

1.  [Hell is Other People (But Mostly Exes)](https://helenhigginsbooks1.substack.com/p/hell-is-other-people-but-mostly-exes) (62 likes): On Sartre, shame, and the people who still know your middle name. By in *Helen Higgins*.
    
2.  [Argentina’s Bailout and the New Digital Extractivism](https://www.technostatecraft.com/p/argentinas-bailout-and-the-new-digital) (9 likes): The fusion of austerity and AI “innovation” turns crisis management into a frontier for U.S. power and corporate profit. By in *Techno-Statecraft*.
    
3.  [“The people of Gaza did not give up, they did not surrender.” - Haidar Eid](https://www.radicalbookscollective.com/p/the-people-of-gaza-did-not-give-up) (20 likes): A conversation Haidar Eid, author of “Decolonizing the Palestinian Mind” and “Banging on the Walls of the Tank: Dispatches from Gaza.”. By in *Radical Books Collective*.
    
4.  [Jesus and the Doomsday Clock, part 1: The Cross and the Sword](https://heyslick.substack.com/p/jesus-and-the-doomsday-clock-1) (14 likes): How Apocalyptic Christianity became America’s new theology of power. By @heyslick in *Hey Slick*.
    
5.  [The Man of the Liberal Consensus](https://cyrilhedoin.substack.com/p/the-man-of-the-liberal-consensus) (9 likes): Some Thoughts About a Recent Biography of Walter Lippmann. By in *The Archimedean Point*.
    
6.  [No more jobs, bitch!](https://beneaththepavement.substack.com/p/no-more-jobs) (54 likes): Ad Astra or no? By in *Beneath the Pavement*.
    
7.  [The Hidden Theurgy of Information Tech](https://thecyberhermetica.substack.com/p/the-hidden-theurgy-of-information) (4 likes): Semiconductors, Binary Code, and the Arcane Art of Digital Modernity. By in *The Cyber Hermetica*.
    
8.  [In the shadow of an empire](https://ashleydelcorno.substack.com/p/in-the-shadow-of-an-empire) (4 likes): power and the American prison system. By in *Truth is a Woman*.
    
9.  [Eight Notes on the Illiterate Universe](https://jason807.substack.com/p/eight-notes-on-the-illiterate-universe) (37 likes): Fragments from the circus of being. By in *The Horn Gate*.
    
10.  [Oscar Wilde on Writing](https://julianphilosophy.substack.com/p/oscar-wilde-on-writing) (100 likes): “We Irish are too poetic to be poets.”. By in *Julian de Medeiros*.
     
11.  [A Story in Two Pictures](https://jaredhenderson.substack.com/p/a-story-in-two-pictures) (127 likes): A self-indulgent personal post. By in *Commonplace Philosophy*.
     
12.  [The relational conception of the self: historical threads, part 2](https://ericalucaststonestreet.substack.com/p/the-relational-conception-of-the-c42) (9 likes): Kropotkin looks at evolution and sees mutual aid as much as competition. By in *Humaning is Hard, but Philosophy Can Help*.
     
13.  [Let the Blackness Roll On: A Travel Diary](https://kellystith.substack.com/p/let-the-blackness-roll-on-a-travel) (5 likes). By in *The Way Out Is Through*.
     
14.  [When Anti-Fascism Becomes Liberal Discipline](https://danieltutt.substack.com/p/when-anti-fascism-becomes-liberal) (23 likes): The Quixote Syndrome and the danger of a left that polices interior life instead of organizing the exploited. By in *Daniel’s Journal*.
     
15.  [The Purpose Of The System](https://peacefulrevolutionary.substack.com/p/the-purpose-of-the-system) (27 likes): The Purpose of a System is What It Does (Not What It Claims to Do). By in *Free Society*.
     
16.  [The New Physics](https://romaricjannel.substack.com/p/the-new-physics) (10 likes): Fritjof Capra, “The Tao of Physics: An Exploration of the Parallels Between Modern Physics and Eastern Mysticism” (1975) #7. By in *Philosophy and Beyond*.
     
17.  [Baphomet Under Glass](https://roguearthistorian.substack.com/p/baphomet-under-glass) (18 likes): #31daysofhalloween. By in *Rogue Art Historian*.
     
18.  [Spinoza Solutions Starts Monday](https://matthewgindin.substack.com/p/spinoza-solutions-starts-monday) (1 likes): a four week, practical dive into the prince of philosophers. By in *Philosophy As Therapy*.
     
19.  [What Tolstoy, Shakespeare, and a Japanese monk teach us about happiness](https://miniphilosophy.substack.com/p/finding-sparks-of-joy) (37 likes): The beauty you find in 2,500-year-old cliche. By and in *Mini Philosophy*.
     
20.  [ICE vs. NYC](https://www.hamiltonnolan.com/p/ice-vs-nyc) (189 likes): The feds will not have a good time here. By in *How Things Work*.
     
21.  [143\. Limerence](https://overthinkpod.substack.com/p/143-limerence) (10 likes). By in *Overthink Podcast*.
     
22.  [AI needs Fascism](https://www.jphilll.com/p/ai-needs-fascism) (235 likes): And Fascism needs AI. By in *New Means*.
     
23.  [The Ouroboros of Intelligence: Inside the $10 Trillion AI Bubble](https://harmoniousdiscourse.substack.com/p/the-ouroboros-of-intelligence-inside) (39 likes). By in *The Structural Lens*.
     
24.  [Andrew on Andrew](https://anro865554.substack.com/p/andrew-on-andrew) (3 likes): An interview with Andrew by Andrew. By in *The* *Existential Pulse*.
     
25.  [Genesis and madness of the big tech billionaire](https://marxandfriends.substack.com/p/genesis-and-madness-of-the-big-tech) (33 likes): From Dot-com revolution to AI bubble and the “antichrist”. By in *Marx and Friends*.
     
26.  [The Worst Play](https://adbusters.substack.com/p/the-worst-play) (10 likes): We showed up. So what? By in *Adbusters*.
     
27.  [Only uncool losers fall in love](https://antoaesthetics.substack.com/p/only-uncool-losers-fall-in-love) (41 likes): On how romance has lost its cool, love became cringe, and why admitting you have a boyfriend is kind of weird. By in *Antoaesthetics*.
     
28.  [Mikaela Loach Loveconomics interview - with text extract in case you prefer reading!](https://laurabasu.substack.com/p/mikaela-loach-on-love-capitalism) (8 likes): The brilliant author of ‘It’s Not That Radical’ joined the dots between climate breakdown, whiteness and capitalism and spoke to me about the wisdom of children. By in *Loveconomics*.
     
29.  [Feminist Stacks Roundup - October 20, 2025](https://feministworks.substack.com/cp/176685499) (7 likes): For your binge-reading pleasure, curated posts from leading feminist stacks. By in *Philosophy Publics*.
     
30.  [Everything I Read During My Philosophy Degree](https://gmwrites.substack.com/p/everything-i-read-during-my-philosophy) (13 likes): On everything I read as part of my BA philosophy degree... By in *g.m.*.
     
31.  [Schopenhauer on love as suffering: Lecture 6, Intimate Relationships](https://substack.com/home/post/p-175750398) (10 likes): Arthur Schopenhauer is philosophy’s favorite pessimist. In The World as Will and Representation, he develops a pseudo-evolutionary account of love that is bound to make everyone mad. By in *Intimate Reltionships*.
     
32.  [Kwesi Thomas](https://celineleboeuf.substack.com/p/kwesi-thomas) (13 likes): On the importance of asking what it means to be human. By and in *Why Philosophy?*.
     
33.  [Chapter One](https://theexistentialreader.substack.com/p/chapter-one) (4 likes): The Fetishisation of Free Speech. By in *The Existential Reader*.
