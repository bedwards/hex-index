##### ROUNDUP

1.  [“Professionals and the Ethics of Workplace Surveillance” - Steve Clarke, William Tuckwell & Morgan Luck](https://newworkinphilosophy.substack.com/p/professionals-and-the-ethics-of-workplace) (10 likes): Journal of Social Philosophy (forthcoming). By in *New Work In Philosophy*.
    
2.  [Sunday notes](https://samantharosehill.substack.com/p/sunday-notes-f4d) (19 likes): poems, clouds, music. By in *Reflections by Samantha Rose Hill*.
    
3.  [The Philosopher’s Browser #11](https://romaricjannel.substack.com/p/the-philosophers-browser-11) (5 likes): Collected Links for the Contemplative Reader. By in *Philosophy and Beyond*.
    
4.  [Embodied Communities Among Those With Hidden Disabilities](https://apablog.substack.com/p/embodied-communities-among-those) (1 likes). By in *Blog of the APA*.
    
5.  [An Existential Guide to: Making Friends](https://theshadowedarchive.substack.com/p/an-existential-guide-to-making-friends) (3722 likes): Friends Friends What Glorious Friendly Friends! By in *The Shadowed Archive*.
    
6.  [Let’s Read Leibniz - Monadology](https://anro865554.substack.com/p/lets-read-leibniz-monadology-043) (4 likes): 13, 14, 15. By in *The Existential Pulse*.
    
7.  [The Flags Return](https://theexistentialreader.substack.com/p/the-flags-return) (5 likes): The Union Jack, St. George’s Flag, and British Identity. By in *The Existential Reader*.
    
8.  [Don’t sacrifice the real world for AI](https://www.jphilll.com/p/dont-sacrifice-the-real-world-for) (202 likes): Data centers, billionaires, and the economic model we live in. By in *New Means*.
    
9.  [Femininity](https://overthinkpod.substack.com/p/femininity) (6 likes). By in *Overthink Podcast*.
    
10.  [Ellie and David Overthink live](https://substack.com/home/post/p-174878397) (11 likes): A recording from Ellie Anderson’s live video. By in *Intimate Reltionships*.
     
11.  [Why do some people crave meaning and others don’t care?](https://miniphilosophy.substack.com/p/why-do-some-people-crave-meaning) (385 likes): Shrugging at the void. By and in *Mini Philosophy*.
     
12.  [Let’s talk about death, baby](https://hellomortal.substack.com/p/lets-talk-about-death-baby) (34 likes): What a year of contemplating mortality has taught us. By in *Hello Mortal*.
     
13.  [Weeping Across Centuries: La Llorona as Myth, Monster, and Muse](https://roguearthistorian.substack.com/p/weeping-across-centuries-la-llorona) (19 likes): Hispanic Heritage Month. By in *Rogue Art Historian*.
     
14.  [How a mathematical move might illuminate a cultural issue](https://ericalucaststonestreet.substack.com/p/how-a-mathematical-move-might-illuminate) (6 likes): Should we ditch gendered vocabulary to undermine patriarchy? By in *Humaning is Hard, but Philosophy Can Help*.
     
15.  [Applying for jobs is so fucking embarrassing](https://beneaththepavement.substack.com/p/applying-for-jobs-is-so-embarrassing) (144 likes): The rituals, the portals, the silence, the begging, the shame. By in *Beneath the Pavement*.
     
16.  [Finding Freedom from the Trance of Unworthiness: A Review of Tara Brach’s Radical Acceptance](https://substack.com/home/post/p-174745687) (6 likes). By in *Masroor Shah*.
     
17.  [The Leverage State: How Regulatory Power Became Cultural Weapon](https://harmoniousdiscourse.substack.com/p/the-leverage-state-how-regulatory) (4 likes). By in *The Structural Lens*.
     
18.  [A Truth About Life](https://matthewgindin.substack.com/p/a-truth-about-loss-and-death) (6 likes): Our own death is but a momentary loss. By in *Philosophy As Therapy*.
     
19.  [Festivities, Conversations, Books, and Podcasts](https://davidlivingstonesmith.substack.com/p/festivities-conversations-books-and) (1 likes). By in *Dehumanization Matters*.
     
20.  [What University is Good For?](https://cyrilhedoin.substack.com/p/what-university-is-good-for) (12 likes): A Liberal Perspective. By in *The Archimedean Point*.
     
21.  [📚 Thinking in Systems - Week 8](https://www.thelivingphilosophy.com/p/thinking-in-systems-week-8) (7 likes): The critique and the end. By in *The Living Philosophy*.
     
22.  [Building Tomorrow Today](https://peacefulrevolutionary.substack.com/p/building-tomorrow-today) (37 likes): How Communities Can Create Alternatives to Capitalism. By in *Free Society*.
     
23.  [A Failed General Failing to be Funny.](https://politicsoflaughter.substack.com/p/a-failed-general-failing-to-be-funny) : When clean-shaven wannabes try to co-opt satire and get it horribly wrong. By in *The Politics of Laughter*.
     
24.  [Ghosted](https://helenhigginsbooks1.substack.com/p/ghosted) (37 likes): From everyday to an empty line, what happens when someone ghosts? By in *Helen Higgins*.
     
25.  [The Inhumanity of Humanism](https://rafaelholmberg.substack.com/p/the-inhumanity-of-humanism) (9 likes): The Cruel Politics of Compassion. By in *Antagonisms of the Everyday: Philosophy, Culture, Politics*.
     
26.  [Why we dread having to take care of our own kids](https://laurabasu.substack.com/p/why-we-dread-taking-care-of-our-own) (34 likes): Don’t worry, it’s not because we’re terrible parents. It’s the nuclear family - capitalism’s thorny little engine room. By in *Loveconomics*.
     
27.  [Love is the Answer to Climate Crisis (Yes, I’m Serious)](https://schoolofattention.substack.com/p/love-is-the-answer-to-climate-crisis) (13 likes): Attention and ecological repair with Henry R. Kramer. By in *The Empty Cup*.
     
28.  [No it’s not about you](https://unionfront.substack.com/cp/174980183) (3 likes): Desire, Alienation, and the Struggle Beyond the Ego-Ideal. By in *Last\_Futurist*.
     
29.  [Leave the Military Now](https://www.hamiltonnolan.com/p/leave-the-military-now) (172 likes): What does courage demand? By in *How Things Work*.
     
30.  [Why Philosophy? Back in October!](https://celineleboeuf.substack.com/p/why-philosophy-back-in-october) (11 likes): Stay tuned for new weekly posts! By in *Why Philosophy?*.
     
31.  [On writing philosophy columns](https://wellreadherring.substack.com/p/on-writing-philosophy-columns) (19 likes): Or “why are the internet men yelling at me again?”. By in *Well-Read Herring*.
     
32.  [I bought an AI Friend](https://jaredhenderson.substack.com/p/i-bought-an-ai-friend) (232 likes): The box sat on my shelf for a full week. By in *Commonplace Philosophy*.
