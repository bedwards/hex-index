##### ROUNDUP

1.  [Maybe we need a Butlerian Jihad after all](https://jaredhenderson.substack.com/p/maybe-we-need-a-butlerian-jihad-after) (269 likes): AI won’t bring your dead loved ones back. By in *Commonplace Philosophy*.
    
2.  [Omer Al Tijani on documenting Sudanese cuisine as an act of resistance](https://www.radicalbookscollective.com/p/omer-al-tijani-on-documenting-sudanese) (13 likes): In a country ravaged by war and where hunger has been weaponized in extreme ways, The Sudanese Kitchen cookbook works against invisibility and erasure. By in *Radical Books Collective*.
    
3.  [148\. Loneliness (extended)](https://overthinkpod.substack.com/p/148-loneliness-extended) (5 likes). By in *Overthink Podcast*.
    
4.  [Reader, He Didn’t Study Me](https://helenhigginsbooks1.substack.com/p/reader-he-didnt-study-me) (71 likes): A quiet letter to the one that didn’t flinch at the draft. By in *Helen Higgins*.
    
5.  [If you learn how to win, you need to learn when to stop.](https://miniphilosophy.substack.com/p/the-genius-and-the-demon) (76 likes): Virtues need virtues to stay virtues. By and in *Mini Philosophy*.
    
6.  [Every Project Is a Tragedy](https://ellismarte.substack.com/p/every-project-is-a-tragedy) (1 likes). By in *Ellis’s Substack*.
    
7.  [Nancy Sherman](https://celineleboeuf.substack.com/p/nancy-sherman) (12 likes): Philosophy teaches us not only intellectual competence, but also healthy emotions and healthy relationships. By and in *Why Philosophy?*.
    
8.  [The Agony of Jealousy](https://julianphilosophy.substack.com/p/the-agony-of-jealousy) (43 likes): “one of the tortures of jealousy is that it can never turn away its eyes from the thing that pains it” -George Eliot. By in *Julian de Medeiros*.
    
9.  [The power of the state](https://www.thepavement.xyz/p/the-power-of-the-state) (33 likes): And how to take (some of) it away. Right now. By in *The Pavement*.
    
10.  [Reasons to be hopeful](https://theexistentialreader.substack.com/p/reasons-to-be-hopeful) (16 likes): The fluidity of social norms and common sense. By in *The Existential Reader*.
     
11.  [Schopen Hour](https://theorygang.substack.com/p/schopen-hour) (4 likes): A recording from a live reading of Arthur Schopenhauer’s Studies on Pessimissm. By in *Theory Gang*.
     
12.  [Twelve Questions For Spinoza, pt 3](https://matthewgindin.substack.com/p/twelve-questions-for-spinoza-pt-3) (2 likes): How are we empowered? Feel better about ourselves? Feel better about the world? By in *Philosophy As Therapy*.
     
13.  [Simone de Beauvoir, The Woman in Love: Lecture 10, Intimate Relationships](https://substack.com/home/post/p-178719629) (5 likes): Why Beauvoir thinks love doesn’t mean the same thing to men and women. By in *Intimate Reltionships*.
     
14.  [Externalities, Rights, and the Problem of Knowledge](https://cyrilhedoin.substack.com/p/externalities-rights-and-the-problem) (18 likes): On the Difficulty of Designing a System of Rights. By in *The Archimedean Point*.
     
15.  [Privacy as Jihad](https://thecyberhermetica.substack.com/p/privacy-as-jihad) (24 likes): A discipline to protect the human principle from algorithmic colonization. By in *The Cyber Hermetica*.
     
16.  [The Method in Hegel’s Self-Censorship](https://danieltutt.substack.com/p/the-method-in-hegels-self-censorship) (12 likes): How to read Hegel’s system vs. his method. Self-censorship and philosophy (Part II). By in *Daniel’s Journal*.
     
17.  [Revisiting the Philosophical Case for Bitcoin](https://apablog.substack.com/p/revisiting-the-philosophical-case) (1 likes): An interview with the authors of Resistance Money: A Philosophical Case for Bitcoin. By in *Blog of the APA*.
     
18.  [Blue Stone, Hard Truths: How Zuni and Diné Jewelry Expose Extraction, Empire, and Appropriation](https://roguearthistorian.substack.com/p/blue-stone-hard-truths-how-zuni-and) (23 likes): #nativeamericanheritagemonth. By in *Rogue Art Historian*.
     
19.  [Epstein Implicates Everyone](https://www.jphilll.com/p/epstein-implicates-everyone) (457 likes): The entire ruling class and the capitalist system are guilty. By in *New Means*.
     
20.  [The Silicon Confessor: Artificial Intelligence and the Future of Human Healing](https://harmoniousdiscourse.substack.com/p/the-silicon-confessor-artificial) (10 likes). By in *The Structural Lens*.
     
21.  [The Trillion-Dollar Vassal](https://www.theorybrief.com/p/the-trillion-dollar-vassal) (8 likes): How Trump 2.0, Israel’s Gaza war, and Norway’s two-trillion-dollar sovereign wealth fund converged in a firestorm of finance, geopolitics, and genocide. By in *The Theory Brief*.
     
22.  [The Ainu Child](https://romaricjannel.substack.com/p/the-ainu-child) (9 likes): From the poet Kitahara Hakushū. By in *Philosophy and Beyond*.
     
23.  [Seeing Without Being Seen](https://kosmotheoros.substack.com/p/seeing-without-being-seen) (4 likes): Gyges’ ring, the face and the Other in Levinas. By in *Kosmotheoros*.
     
24.  [This year, World Philosophy Day = National Absurdity Day](https://ericalucaststonestreet.substack.com/p/this-year-world-philosophy-day-national) (5 likes): Coincidence? By in *Humaning is Hard, but Philosophy Can Help*.
     
25.  [Money And Markets?](https://peacefulrevolutionary.substack.com/p/money-and-markets) (14 likes): Learning About The Liberationists (Satire) - Part 2. By in *Free Society*.
     
26.  [The Ecological Cost of AI Is Much Higher Than You Think](https://reclaimedsystems.substack.com/p/the-ecological-cost-of-ai-is-much) (7 likes). By in *Reclaimed | Systems*.
     
27.  [Notes From Jeffrey Eugenides’s The Virgin Suicides](https://gmwrites.substack.com/p/notes-from-jeffrey-eugenidess-the) (14 likes): On narrative unreliability and self projection... By in *g.m.*.
     
28.  [What disenfranchised grief feels like](https://hellomortal.substack.com/p/what-disenfranchised-grief-feels) (61 likes): How to honor a loss that others may not see or recognize. By in *Hello Mortal*.
     
29.  [Cartographies of Consciousness](https://schoolofattention.substack.com/p/cartographies-of-consciousness) (12 likes): Mapping relational worlds with creative technologist Queenie Wu. By in *The Empty Cup*.
     
30.  [The Philosopher’s Guide to Watching Everything Fall Apart (And What to Do About It) | Part One: Walter Benjamin’s Angel of History](https://philosophypublics.substack.com/p/the-philosophers-guide-to-watching) (6 likes): And the story of Hope, a young whale. By in *Philosophy Publics*.
     
31.  [Trans People Deserve Better](https://wellreadherring.substack.com/p/trans-people-deserve-better) (22 likes): “Gender critical” feminism doesn’t help women but it does help the far right. By in *Well-Read Herring*.
     
32.  [A night off from myself](https://antoaesthetics.substack.com/p/a-night-off-from-myself) (24 likes): On heartbreak, hedonism and the healing power of behaving badly by doing everything wrong on purpose. By in *Antoaesthetics*.
