Hi Frens! 👋 The roundup is getting longer now, so I have two options: **(1)** Curate more narrowly so as to produce a digestible list that will still be useful; or **(2)** create categories as I’ve done below. I’m not sure I’m into the categories, it creates… *problems*. If I narrowed the focus, it would include fewer political commentary and fewer personal essays, even when those have a philosophical bent. Of course, I could also just **(3)** keep the original, all-in-one randomized list. Below I’m trying out categories, but I’d love your feedback on which way to go:

##### ROUNDUP

#### **Philosophy & Ethics**

1.  [Arbitrating absolutism](https://ashleydelcorno.substack.com/p/arbitrating-absolutism) (3 likes): Charlie Kirk, free speech in the US, intention, accountability and conflict. By in *Truth is a Woman*.
    
2.  [142\. Natality with Jennifer Banks (extended)](https://overthinkpod.substack.com/p/142-natality-with-jennifer-banks) (3 likes). By in *Overthink Podcast*.
    
3.  [“Moral Agency without Consciousness” - Jen Semler (Cornell Tech)](https://newworkinphilosophy.substack.com/p/moral-agency-without-consciousness) (11 likes): Canadian Journal of Philosophy, First View (2025). By in *New Work In Philosophy*.
    
4.  [Who’s responsible for the rules?](https://ericalucaststonestreet.substack.com/p/whos-responsible-for-the-rules) (3 likes): A sibling spat stirs up big questions. By in *Humaning is Hard, but Philosophy Can Help*.
    
5.  [Eric Schwitzgebel](https://celineleboeuf.substack.com/p/eric-schwitzgebel) (17 likes): In living life thoughtfully, we are always already practicing philosophy. By in *Why Philosophy?*.
    
6.  [The Postmodern Subject](https://unionfront.substack.com/cp/175359128) (3 likes). By in *Last\_Futurist*.
    
7.  [Something for Someone, Someplace.](https://anro865554.substack.com/p/something-for-someone-someplace) (3 likes): I could add a subtitle but I don’t want to, because this is sooo ironic. By in *The Existential Pulse*.
    
8.  [What is Beyond Language?](https://romaricjannel.substack.com/p/what-is-beyond-language) (14 likes): Fritjof Capra, “The Tao of Physics: An Exploration of the Parallels Between Modern Physics and Eastern Mysticism” (1975) #5. By in *Philosophy and Beyond*.
    
9.  [On The Death of Loved Ones](https://matthewgindin.substack.com/p/on-the-death-of-loved-ones) (4 likes): and Chinese wisdom on the true process of grief. By in *Philosophy As Therapy*.
    
10.  [What, Like It’s Hard?](https://0future.substack.com/p/what-like-its-hard) (3 likes): On Being Based and Adornopilled. By in *No Future*.
     
11.  [why you must write like AI and...](https://theorygang.substack.com/p/why-you-must-write-like-ai-and) (4 likes): how to do it (part 2). By in *Theory Gang*.
     
12.  [Robert Solomon on virtue and erotic love: Lecture 4, Intimate Relationships](https://substack.com/home/post/p-175645238) (8 likes): If erotic love is irrational, does that mean it doesn’t make us better people? By in *Intimate Reltionships*.
     

#### **Social & Political Commentary**

1.  [Thinking of AI as a Social Problem](https://www.hamiltonnolan.com/p/thinking-of-ai-as-a-social-problem) (159 likes): No need to know the unknowable. The knowable is bad enough. By in *How Things Work*.
    
2.  [Rachel Shabi on Gaza, antisemitism and love](https://laurabasu.substack.com/p/rachel-shabi-on-gaza-antisemitism) (13 likes): The brilliant author of ‘Off White’ spoke to me about the political economy of genocide, fighting antisemitism, and how to build loving solidarity within our social movements. By in *Loveconomics*.
    
3.  [Resistance is everywhere — it just isn’t getting enough attention](https://www.jphilll.com/p/resistance-is-happening-everywhere) (391 likes): Nepal, Greece, Indonesia, Italy, Madagascar, and the United States. By in *New Means*.
    
4.  [How we train ourselves to be unkind](https://miniphilosophy.substack.com/p/how-we-train-ourselves-to-be-unkind) (61 likes): The neuroscience of dehumanization. By and in *Mini Philosophy*.
    
5.  [The Fundamentals of Anti-Muslim Hatred](https://www.radicalbookscollective.com/p/the-fundamentals-of-anti-muslim-hatred) (32 likes): Breakdown, design logic and analysis of last week’s quiz titled “Are you prejudiced against Muslims?”. By in *Radical Books Collective*.
    
6.  [Trump’s University Compact demonstrates weakness, not strength](https://www.programmablemutter.com/p/trumps-university-compact-demonstrates) (53 likes): The administration would act differently if it were confident. By in *Programmable Mutter*.
    
7.  [The Dissolution of Civic Infrastructure: Why Individual Freedom Cannot Sustain Complex Societies](https://harmoniousdiscourse.substack.com/p/the-dissolution-of-civic-infrastructure) (38 likes). By in *The Structural Lens*.
    
8.  [Must Work Suck So Much? | Part One](https://philosophypublics.substack.com/p/must-work-suck-so-much-part-one) (10 likes): Kathi Weeks shines a light on our modern work woes. By in *Philosophy Publics*.
    
9.  [The Center’s Political Curse](https://cyrilhedoin.substack.com/p/the-centers-political-curse) (14 likes): On the Failure of Center Politics in France. By in *The Archimedean Point*.
    

#### **Personal Reflection & Culture**

1.  [An Existential Guide to: Making Money](https://theshadowedarchive.substack.com/p/an-existential-guide-to-making-money) (242 likes): moneymoneymoneyMONEY...money... By in *The Shadowed Archive*.
    
2.  [The letter I waited 14 years to open](https://hellomortal.substack.com/p/the-letter-i-waited-14-years-to-open) (73 likes): Written by my mother on her deathbed, to be opened on my wedding day—her words remind us that even after death, it’s possible to show up for the people you love. By in *Hello Mortal*.
    
3.  [Skulls Call Places: Vanitas as Theatre of the Dead](https://roguearthistorian.substack.com/p/skulls-call-places-vanitas-as-theatre) (23 likes): #31DaysofHalloween. By in *Rogue Art Historian*.
    
4.  [I launched a podcast. The first guest is Cory Doctorow.](https://jaredhenderson.substack.com/p/i-launched-a-podcast-the-first-guest) (68 likes): It’s hosted at The Honest Broker and on a new YouTube channel. By in *Commonplace Philosophy*.
    
5.  [Fear and Courage : From Armor to Acceptance](https://substack.com/home/post/p-175253714) (1 likes). By in *Masroor Shah*.
    
6.  [Intimacy is the New Porn](https://helenhigginsbooks1.substack.com/p/intimacy-is-the-new-porn) (97 likes): In an age of exposure, vulnerability is the last taboo. By in *Helen Higgins*.
    
7.  [Who wants to snog me in the designated smoking area tonight](https://antoaesthetics.substack.com/p/who-wants-to-snog-me-in-the-designated) (19 likes): A pub-going participant-observer’s guide to lust, lager and late-night longing in London. By in *Antoaesthetics*.
    
8.  [Notes From R.F.Kuang’s Katabasis](https://gmwrites.substack.com/p/quotes-from-rfkuangs-katabasis) (26 likes): On the state of philosophy and depressed academics... By in *g.m.*.
    
9.  [13 Miles in Search of the Soul of School](https://schoolofattention.substack.com/p/13-miles-in-search-of-the-soul-of) (9 likes): Taking study to the streets with Peter Schmidt. By in *The Empty Cup*.
    
10.  [Never stop feeling](https://julianphilosophy.substack.com/p/never-stop-feeling) (68 likes): On melancholy happiness. By in *Julian de Medeiros*.
     
11.  [The History of Pop 1952 - 1997](https://theexistentialreader.substack.com/p/the-history-of-pop-1952-1997-215) (5 likes): 1965. By in *The Existential Reader*.
     
12.  [Occult or Mainstream? The Singularity Is Silicon Valley’s Religion](https://thecyberhermetica.substack.com/p/occult-or-mainstream-the-singularity) (7 likes): What was once the realm of hooded madmen is now talk-show material: Qabbalah, the Antichrist, and Artificial Intelligence. By in *The Cyber Hermetica*.
