Join us [here](https://open.substack.com/live-stream/75281?utm_source=live-stream-scheduled-upsell)!

To be clear, this is *the* we’re talking about — author of *[Mr. Wilson’s Cabinet of Wonders](https://www.amazon.com/Mr-Wilsons-Cabinet-Wonder-Technology/dp/0679764895)* (1995), *[Vermeer in Bosnia](https://www.penguinrandomhouse.ca/books/188937/vermeer-in-bosnia-by-lawrence-weschler/9780679777403/excerpt)* (2004), *[True to Life: Twenty-Five Years of Conversations with David Hockney](https://www.amazon.com/True-Life-Twenty-Five-Conversations-Hockney/dp/0520258797)* (2008), *[And How Are You, Dr. Sacks? A Biographical Memoir of Oliver Sacks](https://www.amazon.com/How-Are-You-Dr-Sacks/dp/0374236410)* (2019), the supremely enigmatic *[A Trove of Zohars](https://hatandbeard.com/products/a-trove-of-zohars-by-lawrence-weschler)* (2023), etc., etc. Weschler is also author of the wonderful [Wondercabinet](https://lawrenceweschler.substack.com/?utm_campaign=profile_chips) on Substack, to which you should all subscribe. And incidentally he is a great friend and fellow-traveller of *The Hinternet*.

We will be discussing, but not limiting ourselves to, sundry questions related to JSR’s recent essay, “[The Moon Makes Us Human](https://www.the-hinternet.com/p/the-moon-makes-us-human)”. ([Lire en français](https://www.the-hinternet.com/p/cest-la-lune-qui-nous-rend-humains?utm_source=activity_item).) Read it, and join us tomorrow well prepared to throw us a question or two.

And please don’t forget to subscribe to *The Hinternet*! We need your support! This engine of edification runs not on air!
