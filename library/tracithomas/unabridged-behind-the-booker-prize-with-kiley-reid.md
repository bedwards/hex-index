In this bonus episode, I chat with author Kiley Reid for the inside scoop on the [Booker Prize](https://thebookerprizes.com/), an annual literary award given to the best fiction book written in English and published in the UK and Ireland. As a 2025 judge—and the author of a 2020 Booker Prize-longlisted book—Kiley offers insider knowledge on everything from what makes a “Booker book” to the process of narrowing down the list from 153 titles to one winner.
