November was one of my best reading months of the year somehow. Usually by this time of year I am over everything I read, so this was a pleasant surprise. Shout out to my [Brick](https://getbrick.app/thestacks) for helping me stay focused this month, because the desire to scroll can be truly overwhelming.

Below is the little *in case you missed it* section of November episodes of The Stacks. Then for my paid subs, I have all 11 books I read ranked from least to most favorite in a bite-sized 15 minute mini-pod.

\---

### The Stacks November Recap

, founder of [PAGEBREAK](https://www.pagebreak.nyc/), a reading retreat company, joined the show to talk about reading aloud and in community.

One of a my favorite memoirs this year was *[The Hollow Half](https://bookshop.org/a/3111/9781646222438)* by Sarah Aziza, so getting to speak with her about it was totally fulfilling.

Discussing the worlds of play vs novel writing with Pulitzer-winner Quiara Alegría Hudes, author of *[The White Hot](https://bookshop.org/a/3111/9780593732335)*, was an absolute delight and so fascinating.

I realized I had never actually introduced you all to my team here at The Stacks, welp, in November that changed. This bonus episode takes you behind the scenes and into the worlds of my amazing team here. I love them and I think you will, too!

The Stacks Book Club pick in November was *[We the Animals](https://bookshop.org/a/3111/9780547844190)* by Justin Torres and and I loved it and loved talking about it.
