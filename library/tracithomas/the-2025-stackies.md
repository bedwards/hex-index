The Stacks has it’s very own literary awards, [The Stackies](https://www.thestackspodcast.com/book-talk/2024-stackies-winners), and they’re the best. This is not up for debate. What is up for debate? Who the winners will be for 2025. In fact, nominations are now open for this year’s Stackies.

How does it work?

If you’re a paid subscriber on Substack or a member of [The Stacks Pack](https://www.patreon.com/thestacks) you get to nominate books for the prize starting today, and then vote on the winners in January.

Let the bookish rumpus begin!
