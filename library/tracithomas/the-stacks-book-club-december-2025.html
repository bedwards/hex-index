Clear eyes, full hearts, can’t lose. For this month’s book club, we’re diving into a classic sports read: Friday Night Lights: A Town, a Team, and a Dream by Pulitzer Prize winner, H.G. Bissinger. This nonfiction gem follows the 1988 season of the Permian Panthers, one of the best high-school football teams in Texas history. But this story is about much more than just the team—it also explores the town’s football-obsessed culture and the ways that the Panthers impact the community around them. Friday Night Lights was named Sports Illustrated’s best football book of all time, was a #1 New York Times bestseller, and, of course, inspired the acclaimed 2004 film and a wildly popular 2006 TV series of the same name. Are you ready for some football?
