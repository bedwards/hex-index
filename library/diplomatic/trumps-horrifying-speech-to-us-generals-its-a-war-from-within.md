President Trump made clear in a horrifying speech to U.S. generals today (Sept. 30) that he plans to use the U.S. military primarily to quell protests and fight “the enemy within” in American cities led by elected Democrats. Much as he has recently enlisted the Justice Department to persecute his political enemies.

“Last month, I signed an executive order to provide training for a quick reaction force, that can help quell civil disturbances,” Trump [told](https://www.sofx.com/president-trump-address-to-pentagon-leaders-at-marine-corps-base-quantico-full-transcript/) several hundred U.S. generals flown in from around the world for the unprecedented meeting, held at the US Marine Corps base in Quantico, Virginia.

“This is going to be important for the people in this room, because it’s the enemy from within, and we have to handle it before it gets out of control,” he said. “It won’t get out of control once you get involved.”

***Trump says U.S. cities should be military “training ground”***

Trump said he told Secretary of Defense Pete Hegseth they should use American cities as a “training ground” for the U.S. military, particularly in cities and states with elected Democratic leaders that Trump does not like, or who dare criticize Trump.

“And I told Pete we should use some of these dangerous cities as training grounds for our military — National Guard, but military — because we’re going into Chicago very soon,” Trump said. “That’s a big city with an incompetent governor. Stupid governor.”

Illinois Governor J.B. Pritzker is “always up there saying, ‘We’re in very good shape. We don’t need the military,’” Trump told the gathering at Quantico. “No, they need the military desperately. How about Portland? Portland, Oregon, where it looks like a war zone.”

“We have many cities in great shape, too, by the way,” Trump said. “But it seems that the ones that are run by the radical-left Democrats — what they’ve done to San Francisco, Chicago, New York, Los Angeles — they’re very unsafe places, and we’re going to straighten them out one by one.”

“And this is going to be a major part for some of the people in this room,” Trump said. “That’s a war, too. It’s a war from within.”

***‘We don’t want you fighting wars’ abroad***

Trump told the generals that he did not plan to use the U.S. military to fight wars in the Middle East or abroad.

“We want to save you for other things, or save you for nothing,” he said. “From that standpoint, we don’t want you fighting wars.”

He wants to primarily use the military at home.

“America is under invasion from within,” Trump, standing before an enormous US flag. “We’re under invasion from within. No different than a foreign enemy, but more difficult in many ways because they don’t wear uniforms.”

***Rep. Moulton: US cities should never be military ‘training ground’***

From video clips of the address, and pool reports by reporters in attendance, there seemed to be notably minimal public response from the stoic faced U.S. generals in attendance, a good many of whom had to be privately disturbed.

But US Congressman and former Marine Seth Moulton (D-Mass.) expressed alarm and disgust.

“U.S. cities should never be ‘training grounds’ for the military,” Moulton [wrote](https://x.com/sethmoulton/status/1973050219295130090?s=58&t=8ajOAkN9LQ_NZJJQCFBh2g) on Twitter. “There is no ‘enemy from within.’”

“The reputational and operational damage being done to our military will take years to undo.”
