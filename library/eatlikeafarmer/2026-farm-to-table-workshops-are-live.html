Hey friends, I’ve got some exciting news to share… my calendar of 2026 farm to table workshops is officially live!

My workshops are 100% hands-on, include a full tour of the farm, and everyone helps harvest veggies, fruit, and herbs that we’ll use to make (and eat!) several vibrant, seasonal dishes together… all while learning tons of handy gardening and culinary lessons. It really doesn’t get more *farm to table* than this.

Classes take place outside between the garden and our covered workshop space, with a max size of 10 people to ensure an intimate and safe experience for everyone. Our 10-acre organic farm is super easy to get to, and is located 15 minutes west of Carrboro and 5 minutes down the road from the sweet village of Saxapahaw.

For the past 4 years, all of my workshops have been 100% sold out, so if you’re hoping to join us next year, now is the time to sign up!
