Alright, let’s get to this week’s new recipe, which is an absolute must-have at Thanksgiving (which… just to remind you/me… is next week)!

In my family, it’s simply not Thanksgiving without sweet potato casserole. This recipe has been passed through the generations from my fabulous Great-Aunt Shirlee (who actually lives just down the road from us in Hickory, NC), and it truly is one of my all time favorite holiday dishes to make (and eat)!

Now, I know there are two different camps when it comes to the proper topping on a sweet potato casserole: team marshmallow and team pecan. Not trying to ruffle any feathers here, but I truly *cannot fathom* putting sweet/soft marshmallows on top of an already sweet/soft sweet potato casserole?! Someone please make it make sense.

All to say, I am obviously on team pecan, as it is clearly the optimal topping for giving the casserole some much needed texture and crunch. Another bonus, the topping comes together in about 5 minutes, which you can easily whip up while the sweet potatoes are in the oven.

I’d like to think this sweet potato casserole strikes the perfect balance of sweet and savory, and I love how decadent it tastes without being cloyingly sweet.

Whatever team you’re on, I genuinely hope you have a joy-filled and sweet potato-filled Thanksgiving holiday. Happy cookin good lookin!
