Side note… this would be perfect for feeding hungry house guests over the holidays! As written, the recipe serves 4 hungry people, but it can easily be doubled to feed a crowd.

A few quick ingredient notes:

  • Fresh tortellini is a revelation! Very affordable, feeds a crowd, and cooks in 5 minutes. Look for it in the refrigerated section, usually wherever your grocery store has specialty cheeses/cured meats/dips. This is a brand that I especially like.

  • The sun-dried tomatoes make this recipe SHINE, so don’t skip them! You’ll need about 1 cup of drained and sliced sun-dried tomatoes, which I find is about the contents of a 7-ounce jar (like this one).

  • Just a friendly reminder that ALL of my recipes are seasoned exclusively with Diamond kosher salt, and I *highly* recommend stocking your kitchen with this brand of salt for all your cooking, which is the industry gold standard. If you’re using fine sea salt or any kind of table salt, cut the amount of salt written in this recipe in half.
