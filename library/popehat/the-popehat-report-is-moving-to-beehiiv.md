As I mentioned before, the Popehat Report is moving to Beehiiv, a different platform for blogs and newsletters. You can find it at [www.popehat.com](https://www.popehat.com/).   The next email you get from me should be from there.  Everyone's subscriptions should transfer over. The first time I sent an email from there, in a few minutes, it might go to your spam, so you may want to check that. I will be posting links to everything on [Bluesky](https://bsky.app/profile/kenwhite.bsky.social), [Threads](https://www.threads.net/@kenpopehat), and [Facebook](https://www.facebook.com/profile.php?id=100057614584451) so you'll know if you're missing something.  You'll be able to subscribe (or unsubscribe, if your time of tolerating me is coming to a middle) just as easily there.

I made this decision for a number of reasons, only one of which is related to recent controversy.  As I said before, I don't judge anyone for deciding to stay on Substack, and I think a preference for a hands-off moderation stance is one of many reasonable approaches to complex and intractable problems.  To the contrary, I find the denunciations of people who stay here (or who leave) to be contrived and unreasonable.  In fact, I will still be on Substack as a co-host on Serious Trouble, which is hosted here, and I'm fine with that.  For my own personal blog, this is what's right for me.   I've changed platforms a dozen times writing under the Popehat name and may live to change a dozen more.  The new platform will let me make some design changes and be a little more "bloggy," for want of a better word.

Watch for the first Beehiiv post to follow this one today in a short while.
