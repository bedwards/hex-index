# **[Jennifer Abruzzo Wants Workers to Fight Back](https://workdaymagazine.org/jennifer-abruzzo-wants-workers-to-fight-back/)**

Read our exclusive conversation with the former head of the NLRB covering everything from protesting about Gaza in the workplace to taking on multinational companies challenging the constitutionality of the NLRA:

> “I do feel that this is a moment and an opportunity for workers to speak up and out about their pain points and their issues at this moment in time, and to seek accountability and to go and seek support from the court of public opinion, and to use their power to withhold labor, if that’s what it takes for them to be heard and for changes to be made that’s going to inure the benefit of them and their families and the communities."
