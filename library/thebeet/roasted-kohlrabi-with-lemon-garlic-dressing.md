### Ingredients

-   3 medium kohlrabi bulbs, peeled and diced into 1-inch cubes
    
-   2 tablespoons olive oil
    
-   1 teaspoon sea salt
    
-   ½ teaspoon freshly cracked black pepper
    
-   1 clove garlic, minced
    
-   Zest and juice of 1 lemon
    
-   1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley (or dill)
    
-   Optional: a sprinkle of grated Parmesan or nutritional yeast for serving
    

\---

### Instructions

1.  **Prep the kohlrabi**: Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Peel off the tough outer layer of each kohlrabi bulb and dice into cubes.
    
2.  **Season & roast**: Toss the cubes in olive oil, salt, and pepper. Spread evenly on a baking sheet and roast for 25–30 minutes, flipping halfway, until golden and tender.
    
3.  **Make the dressing**: In a small bowl, whisk together garlic, lemon zest, lemon juice, and parsley.
    
4.  **Finish**: When the kohlrabi comes out of the oven, drizzle with the lemon-garlic dressing. Toss gently to coat.
    
5.  **Serve**: Top with Parmesan or nutritional yeast if desired. Serve warm as a side dish or toss into a grain bowl.
