### Ingredients

-   2 medium kohlrabi bulbs, peeled and shredded (or julienned)
    
-   1 crisp apple (like Honeycrisp or Gala), julienned
    
-   1 carrot, shredded
    
-   2 green onions, thinly sliced
    
-   2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
    
-   1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
    
-   1 teaspoon honey (or maple syrup for vegan)
    
-   3 tablespoons olive oil
    
-   Salt and pepper to taste
    
-   2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley or dill
    
-   Optional: toasted sunflower seeds or pumpkin seeds for crunch
    

\---

### Instructions

1.  **Prep vegetables**: Peel the kohlrabi and cut into thin matchsticks or shred with a box grater. Do the same with the apple and carrot. Place in a large bowl with green onions.
    
2.  **Make the vinaigrette**: In a small jar or bowl, whisk vinegar, mustard, honey/maple, olive oil, salt, and pepper until smooth.
    
3.  **Toss together**: Pour vinaigrette over the slaw and mix well. Let sit 10 minutes for flavors to meld.
    
4.  **Finish & serve**: Sprinkle with fresh herbs and seeds before serving.
